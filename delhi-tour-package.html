  • Day 1

    Arrival & Evening City Drive

    Arrive in Delhi and enjoy a smooth airport or station transfer to your hotel. In the evening, witness the illuminated India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, and Parliament House, followed by a stroll through Connaught Place. Overnight stay in Delhi.

  • Day 2

    Old Delhi – A Walk Through Time

    Dive into Mughal grandeur at the Red Fort and Jama Masjid. Ride a rickshaw through Chandni Chowk, taste authentic street food, and visit Raj Ghat, Gandhi’s memorial. Optional evening cultural show or food trail.

  • Day 3

    New Delhi – Architecture, Museums & Gardens

    Explore Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, and the peaceful Lotus Temple. Visit the National Museum or Crafts Museum, then unwind at Lodhi Garden or Agrasen ki Baoli. End the day shopping at Dilli Haat or dining at a rooftop restaurant.

  • Day 4

    Departure – Until Next Time, Delhi

    After breakfast, check out and visit Akshardham Temple (optional) before transfer to airport/railway station.
