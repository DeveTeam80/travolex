  • Day 1

    Arrival in Delhi – Welcome to the Capital

    Your journey begins with your arrival in Delhi, where Travolex provides a comfortable pickup from the airport or railway station and transfers you to your centrally located hotel. After check-in and a brief rest, step out for an introductory evening drive past the India Gate, Parliament House, and Rashtrapati Bhavan, beautifully lit up after sunset. If time allows, enjoy a relaxing walk at Connaught Place and explore its colonial charm, café culture, and boutique shops. Return to your hotel for a delicious dinner and settle in for the night, ready to dive deep into Delhi’s rich layers of history the following day.

  • Day 2

    Old Delhi – A Walk Through Time

    Begin your day with a hearty breakfast before setting off on a guided exploration of Old Delhi. Visit the majestic Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that served as the seat of Mughal power. From there, walk or take a rickshaw ride through the vibrant lanes of Chandni Chowk, experiencing its bustling bazaars, spice markets, and traditional eateries. Stop by the grand Jama Masjid, one of India’s largest mosques, and later visit Raj Ghat, the serene memorial of Mahatma Gandhi. In the afternoon, explore the iconic Khari Baoli spice market or opt for a curated street food trail. Return to your hotel by evening to relax or enjoy a cultural performance or light-and-sound show (optional add-on).

  • Day 3

    New Delhi – Architecture, Museums & Gardens

    Today is dedicated to the elegant expanse of New Delhi. Start your morning with a visit to the towering Qutub Minar and the serene Humayun’s Tomb, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture. Next, explore the Lotus Temple, famous for its unique flower-like design and meditative ambiance. After lunch at a local restaurant, head to the National Museum or the Crafts Museum to delve into India’s vast cultural heritage. Wrap up the afternoon at Lodhi Garden or Agrasen ki Baoli for a quiet stroll amidst history and greenery. In the evening, you can shop for handicrafts and souvenirs at Dilli Haat or take it easy with dinner at a rooftop restaurant offering city views. Overnight stay at your hotel.

  • Day 4

    Departure – Until Next Time, Delhi

    On your final day, enjoy a relaxed breakfast and check out from your hotel. If time permits, Travolex can arrange a quick stop at Akshardham Temple for its grand architecture and peaceful gardens before your drop-off at the airport or railway station. As your Delhi adventure concludes, you leave with a deeper connection to India’s history, culture, and vibrant urban spirit.
