Sanur Bali Travel Guide: 24 Best Things To Do

22 November

Sanur, located on Bali's southeastern coast, is a serene seaside town perfect for travelers looking for a mix of relaxation, cultural exploration, and adventure. Unlike the bustling scenes of Kuta or Seminyak, Sanur offers a quieter, family-friendly atmosphere while still providing plenty of exciting activities and attractions. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, this guide covers the 24 best things to do in Sanur to make your

Bali trip

unforgettable.

1. Relax on Sanur Beach


Sanur Beach is the heart of this town, offering pristine sands, calm waters, and a peaceful vibe. Unlike the wilder waves of other Bali beaches, Sanur's waters are perfect for swimming and family-friendly activities.
Tip: Book a sun lounger and enjoy the sunrise, as Sanur is famous for its spectacular morning views.



2. Enjoy the Sunrise Walk on the Promenade


The Sanur Beach Promenade stretches for 5 kilometers along the coastline. Walking or cycling along this scenic path at sunrise is one of the best ways to experience Sanur's beauty.
Travolex offers bike rentals and guided morning tours along the promenade.



3. Visit the Le Mayeur Museum


Art enthusiasts will love the Le Mayeur Museum, dedicated to Belgian artist Adrien-Jean Le Mayeur. Set in a traditional Balinese house, the museum displays the artist's stunning works inspired by Bali's landscapes and culture.



4. Try Watersports at Sanur Beach

From kayaking and paddleboarding to jet skiing, Sanur Beach offers a variety of watersport activities. The calm sea makes it an ideal location for beginners.



5. Explore Bali's Turtle Conservation and Education Center

Located nearby on Serangan Island, this conservation center rescues, rehabilitates, and releases turtles back into the wild. Learn about their efforts and even release a baby turtle into the sea.



6. Take a Day Trip to Nusa Penida


Sanur is the main departure point for ferries to Nusa Penida, a stunning island famous for its rugged cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking beaches like Kelingking Beach.
Travolex can organize a day trip or overnight stay to Nusa Penida with transport and guides.



7. Shop at Sanur Night Market (Pasar Sindhu)


For an authentic local experience, visit Pasar Sindhu in the evening. This lively night market offers traditional Balinese food, fresh fruits, and handmade souvenirs at reasonable prices.



8. Enjoy a Traditional Balinese Massage


Sanur has plenty of wellness centers and spas offering rejuvenating Balinese massages. Treat yourself to a relaxing spa session with natural oils and techniques that relieve stress.



9. Visit the Blanjong Pillar


The Blanjong Pillar is Bali's oldest inscription, dating back to the 10th century. This ancient artifact is an important historical site and offers a glimpse into Bali's rich cultural past.



10. Dine at Beachfront Cafes and Restaurants


Sanur is home to a variety of beachfront restaurants where you can enjoy fresh seafood, international dishes, and local Balinese cuisine. Try cafes like Soul in a Bowl and Malaika Secret Moksha.



11. Take a Cooking Class


Learn the secrets of Balinese cuisine by joining a cooking class in Sanur. You'll get hands-on experience preparing traditional dishes like Nasi Goreng, Satay, and Balinese curries.
Travolex can arrange cooking classes with professional chefs.



12. Discover Serangan Island


Just a short drive from Sanur, Serangan Island is a hidden gem where you can visit turtle sanctuaries, relax on quiet beaches, and enjoy local food.



13. Go Snorkeling or Diving at Sanur Reef


Sanur offers great snorkeling and diving opportunities, with its coral reef located just a short boat ride from the shore. Explore vibrant marine life and colorful coral formations.



14. Experience the Bali Kite Festival


Sanur hosts the annual Bali Kite Festival during the dry season (July-August). Watch massive traditional kites soar through the skies, accompanied by cultural performances.



15. Relax at Sindhu Beach


Sindhu Beach is another peaceful spot within Sanur, known for its laid-back vibe and stunning sunsets. It's perfect for a quiet evening stroll or a beachside dinner.



16. Visit the Big Garden Corner


This unique open-air art gallery and garden is ideal for families and photography enthusiasts. It features intricate statues, art installations, and colorful gardens.



17. Go Fishing with Local Fishermen


Sanur's traditional fishing culture remains alive. Join local fishermen for an early morning fishing tour and learn their time-honored techniques.



18. Try Yoga at a Beachfront Studio


Sanur has several yoga studios offering beachfront classes. Start your day with a relaxing yoga session surrounded by the calming sounds of the ocean.



19. Explore Sanur's Street Art


Discover Sanur's creative side by exploring its street art and murals. Many alleys and hidden corners feature colorful artworks that reflect Balinese culture.



20. Take a Day Trip to Ubud


Sanur's location makes it easy to visit Ubud, Bali's cultural hub. Explore the Ubud Monkey Forest, Tegalalang Rice Terraces, and the vibrant Ubud Art Market.
Travolex offers hassle-free day trips to Ubud from Sanur.



21. Visit Pura Segara Temple


Pura Segara is one of Sanur's most prominent temples. Located near the sea, it offers a peaceful atmosphere perfect for reflection and appreciation of Balinese spirituality.



22. Spend Time at Mertasari Beach


Mertasari Beach is popular for watersports, jogging, and kite flying. Its relaxed vibe makes it ideal for travelers looking to escape the crowds.



23. Stay in a Beachfront Villa


Sanur has plenty of luxury beachfront villas that offer comfort and stunning ocean views. It's the perfect way to enjoy Bali's coastal charm.

24. Explore Local Art Galleries


Sanur's local art galleries feature works by talented Balinese artists, showcasing everything from traditional paintings to contemporary art pieces.
