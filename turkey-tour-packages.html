  • Day 1

    Arrival in Istanbul

    Arrive in Istanbul, where East meets West. Meet your Travolex representative and transfer to your hotel. In the evening, enjoy an optional Bosphorus Dinner Cruise with live Turkish music and folk performances.
  • Day 2

    Istanbul Historical Discovery

    Explore Istanbul’s Old City highlights: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, and the Grand Bazaar. Walk through Hippodrome Square and immerse yourself in the city’s imperial past.
  • Day 3

    Flight to Cappadocia – Cave Villages & Valleys

    Fly to Cappadocia, known for its surreal rock formations. Visit the Goreme Open-Air Museum, Pasabag Valley, and Avanos pottery workshops. Stay overnight in a charming cave hotel.
  • Day 4

    Hot Air Balloon Ride & Underground Cities

    Start your day with an optional Cappadocia Hot Air Balloon Ride at sunrise. Visit Kaymakli Underground City, hike the Ihlara Valley, and enjoy a Turkish Night Show with traditional music and food.
  • Day 5

    Flight to Izmir – Transfer to Kusadasi

    Fly to Izmir and transfer to Kusadasi, a coastal gem by the Aegean Sea. Enjoy leisure time by the beach or marina with dinner at a seaside restaurant.
  • Day 6

    Day Trip to Ephesus & House of Virgin Mary

    Visit Ephesus, one of the best-preserved ancient Roman cities. Explore the Library of Celsus, Great Theater, and Temple of Hadrian. Later, visit the House of the Virgin Mary, a revered pilgrimage site.
  • Day 7

    Pamukkale & Hierapolis Excursion

    Discover Pamukkale’s white travertine pools and the ruins of Hierapolis. Enjoy a swim in Cleopatra’s Pool, surrounded by ancient columns. Return to Kusadasi for your final night.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Izmir or Istanbul

    After breakfast, transfer to Izmir or Istanbul Airport for your flight home, filled with unforgettable memories of Turkey’s beauty, history, and warm hospitality.
