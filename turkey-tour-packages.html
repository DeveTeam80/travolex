  • Day 1

    Arrival in Istanbul

    Arrive in Istanbul, the city where East meets West, and meet your Travolex representative for a warm welcome and private transfer to your hotel. After check-in, spend the remainder of the day at leisure. In the evening, enjoy an optional Bosphorus dinner cruise with live Turkish music and folk performances as you float between two continents under the city’s twinkling lights.

  • Day 2

    Istanbul Historical Discovery

    Begin your day with a full-day guided tour of Istanbul’s Old City. Visit the iconic Hagia Sophia, a former basilica and mosque that now stands as a museum of layered history. Continue to the stunning Blue Mosque, famous for its six minarets and Iznik tiles, then walk through the ancient Hippodrome Square, once the heart of Byzantine civic life. After lunch, explore Topkapi Palace, the opulent residence of Ottoman sultans, and end the day at the Grand Bazaar, one of the oldest and largest covered markets in the world. Return to your hotel or continue your evening exploring the cafés and restaurants in Sultanahmet.
  • Day 3

    Flight to Cappadocia – Cave Villages & Valleys

    After an early breakfast, transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Cappadocia, a region known for its surreal landscapes and fairy chimneys. Upon arrival, begin exploring the unique topography with a visit to Goreme Open Air Museum, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring rock-hewn churches with beautiful frescoes. Visit Pasabag (Monks Valley) to see the iconic mushroom-shaped rock formations and stop at a local pottery workshop in Avanos. In the evening, check in to your boutique cave hotel and enjoy dinner in a charming local restaurant built into the stone.
  • Day 4

    Hot Air Balloon Ride & Underground Cities

    Start the day early with an optional hot air balloon ride, one of the most iconic experiences in Turkey, as you glide over the valleys and volcanic landscapes at sunrise. After breakfast, descend into the mysterious Kaymakli or Derinkuyu Underground City, once used by early Christians as a refuge from invaders. Later, hike through the lush Ihlara Valley or visit Uchisar Castle, the highest point in Cappadocia. Return to your hotel for a relaxed evening or enjoy a Turkish Night Show with folk dances and regional cuisine in a traditional cave venue.
  • Day 5

    Flight to Izmir – Transfer to Kusadasi

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Izmir, located on Turkey’s western coast. Upon arrival, you’ll be transferred to the resort town of Kusadasi, a popular base for exploring ancient cities and seaside beauty. After hotel check-in, enjoy a relaxed afternoon by the Aegean Sea or take a sunset walk along the Kusadasi marina. Dinner will be served at a seaside restaurant, where you can sample fresh seafood and Aegean-style mezze.
  • Day 6

    Day Trip to Ephesus & House of Virgin Mary

    Today you’ll explore one of the most extraordinary ancient cities in the world—Ephesus. Walk through marble streets once walked by emperors, visit the Library of Celsus, the Great Theater, and the Temple of Hadrian, gaining insight into life during Roman times. Continue to the House of the Virgin Mary, a revered pilgrimage site located on a peaceful hillside. Return to Kusadasi in the afternoon and enjoy some time for shopping or relaxing by the sea.
  • Day 7

    Pamukkale & Hierapolis Excursion

    After an early breakfast, take a day trip to the natural wonder of Pamukkale, famous for its terraced white travertine pools created by calcium-rich thermal waters. Visit the ancient city of Hierapolis, where you can explore the ruins of baths, temples, and a grand theater. You may choose to take a dip in the historic Cleopatra’s Pool, where ancient columns lie submerged in mineral water. In the evening, return to Kusadasi for your final night on the Aegean coast.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Izmir or Istanbul

    After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Izmir Airport for your flight home. Depending on your international departure, a connecting flight to Istanbul may be arranged. Leave Turkey with unforgettable memories of ancient empires, unique landscapes, and warm hospitality.
