Top 10 Places to Visit in June in India

15 February

June in India marks the onset of the monsoon season in many parts of the country, bringing relief from the intense summer heat. This time of year is perfect for exploring the diverse landscapes and vibrant cultures that India has to offer. Here are ten fantastic

destinations to travel in India

in the month of June .


Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir


Known for its dramatic landscapes and serene monasteries, Ladakh is a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers. June is an ideal time to visit as the roads from Manali and Srinagar open up, revealing stunning views of snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and picturesque valleys.



Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Manali offers a refreshing retreat with its cool climate and scenic beauty. Whether you're interested in trekking, river rafting, or simply enjoying the lush greenery, Manali has something for everyone. Rohtang Pass, which opens up in June, is a must-visit for snow lovers.



Coorg, Karnataka


Known as the Scotland of India, Coorg is famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, and lush forests. June is an excellent time to visit Coorg to experience the region’s natural beauty at its peak, with the beginning of the monsoon adding a magical touch to the landscapes.



Munnar, Kerala

Munnar is a stunning hill station in Kerala(h2), renowned for its sprawling tea gardens and pleasant weather. In June, the monsoon transforms Munnar into a lush green paradise, making it a perfect destination for nature enthusiasts and honeymooners.



Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, offers a blend of colonial charm and natural beauty. The pleasant weather in June makes it an ideal time for sightseeing, leisurely strolls on the Mall Road, and exploring the nearby Kufri and Naldehra.



Darjeeling, West Bengal


Famous for its tea plantations and panoramic views of the Kanchenjunga range, Darjeeling is a delightful destination in June. The monsoon showers enhance the beauty of its landscapes, making it an excellent time for tea garden tours and heritage train rides.



Shillong, Meghalaya


Shillong, known as the Scotland of the East, is a picturesque hill station with a cool, refreshing climate. June is a great time to visit Shillong to experience its waterfalls, lush green hills, and vibrant local culture. Don’t miss the stunning views from Shillong Peak and Elephant Falls.



Ooty, Tamil Nadu


Ooty, or Udhagamandalam, is a charming hill station in Tamil Nadu, famous for its beautiful gardens, rolling hills, and pleasant climate. In June, the misty mornings and blooming flowers make it an ideal destination for a relaxing getaway.



Rishikesh, Uttarakhand


Rishikesh is the perfect destination for those seeking spiritual solace or adventure. Known as the Yoga Capital of the World, it offers a serene environment for yoga and meditation. June is also a great time for river rafting and trekking in the nearby hills.



Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh


Tawang, with its stunning landscapes and rich Buddhist culture, is a hidden gem in Northeast India. June is a good time to visit as the weather is relatively pleasant, and you can explore the Tawang Monastery, serene lakes, and beautiful valleys.
