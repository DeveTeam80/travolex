  • Day 1

    Arrival in Beijing

    Arrive at Beijing International Airport, meet your Travolex representative, and transfer to your hotel. Evening free to explore Wangfujing Street or enjoy authentic Peking duck.

  • Day 2

    Beijing Historical Tour

    Visit Tiananmen Square, the grand Forbidden City, and the Temple of Heaven. End the day with an optional Peking Opera or Kung Fu show.
  • Day 3

    Great Wall Excursion

    Explore the Mutianyu section of the Great Wall, known for stunning mountain views. Visit a jade factory and enjoy a Hutong rickshaw tour in old Beijing.
  • Day 4

    Beijing to Xi’an via High-Speed Train

    Travel aboard China’s modern high-speed rail to Xi’an, the heart of the ancient Silk Road. Explore the Muslim Quarter and enjoy local street food in the evening.
  • Day 5

    Terracotta Warriors & Ancient Wonders

    Witness the legendary Terracotta Army, then visit a pottery workshop. Cycle atop the Xi’an City Wall and enjoy an optional Tang Dynasty dinner show.
  • Day 6

    Xi’an to Shanghai by Flight

    Fly to Shanghai, China’s futuristic city. Stroll along The Bund, blending colonial charm with modern architecture, and enjoy a Huangpu River night cruise.
  • Day 7

    Shanghai City Tour

    Visit Yu Garden, Jade Buddha Temple, and the Shanghai Museum. In the afternoon, head to Pudong for panoramic views from the Shanghai Tower or Oriental Pearl TV Tower.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Shanghai

    After breakfast, transfer to Pudong International Airport for your flight home. Optional last-minute shopping or coffee by the skyline before departure.
