  • Day 1

    Arrival in Beijing

    Upon arrival at Beijing Capital International Airport, a Travolex representative will greet you and assist with your transfer to the hotel. After check-in, you have the rest of the day to relax and recover from your journey. If time permits, you can enjoy a peaceful walk in the nearby Wangfujing shopping street or opt for a traditional Peking duck dinner in a local restaurant.

  • Day 2

    Beijing Historical Tour

    After breakfast, begin your journey through China’s imperial past with a full-day guided tour of Beijing. Start with a visit to the majestic Tiananmen Square, followed by the Forbidden City—the largest palace complex in the world and home to 24 emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties. After lunch, explore the Temple of Heaven, where ancient emperors performed rituals to ensure good harvests. In the evening, enjoy a cultural performance like the Peking Opera or a kung fu show, based on your interests.
  • Day 3

    Great Wall Excursion

    Today is dedicated to one of the world’s greatest architectural feats—the Great Wall of China. Travel to the Mutianyu section, known for its beautiful surroundings and fewer crowds. You can ascend by cable car, walk along the wall’s ancient pathways, and enjoy panoramic views of the mountains. Afterward, visit a local jade factory en route back to the city. In the evening, stroll through the narrow alleys of the Hutongs for an optional rickshaw tour and enjoy dinner in a traditional courtyard home.
  • Day 4

    Beijing to Xi’an via High-Speed Train

    After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to the train station for a thrilling high-speed rail journey to Xi’an, the ancient capital and eastern terminus of the Silk Road. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel and take the rest of the day to explore the nearby Muslim Quarter, known for its street food and vibrant local markets. For dinner, sample some of Xi’an’s famous hand-pulled noodles or dumplings.
  • Day 5

    Terracotta Warriors & Ancient Wonders

    Begin the day with a visit to one of China’s greatest archaeological discoveries—the Terracotta Army, a vast underground army built to protect Emperor Qin Shi Huang in the afterlife. After exploring this UNESCO World Heritage Site, visit a local pottery workshop to learn how replicas are made. Return to Xi’an in the afternoon and visit the Ancient City Wall, where you can choose to cycle along the top of the well-preserved fortifications. The evening is yours to explore at leisure or enjoy a Tang Dynasty cultural show with dinner.
  • Day 6

    Xi’an to Shanghai by Flight

    After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Shanghai, China’s modern metropolis. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and enjoy a relaxing afternoon. You can take a gentle walk along the historic Bund, with its colonial-era architecture on one side and the futuristic skyline of Pudong on the other. In the evening, consider a Huangpu River cruise to witness the city’s dazzling lights from the water.
  • Day 7

    Shanghai City Tour

    Today’s full-day tour begins with a visit to the peaceful Yu Garden and the bustling Yuyuan Bazaar. Next, explore the Shanghai Museum, renowned for its ancient Chinese art collection, followed by a visit to Jade Buddha Temple, an active place of worship with intricate statues. In the afternoon, head to Pudong to explore modern marvels like the Shanghai Tower or the Oriental Pearl TV Tower, both offering sweeping city views. Spend your final evening shopping along Nanjing Road or enjoying dinner in the ultra-chic Xintiandi district.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Shanghai

    After breakfast, check out and transfer to Shanghai Pudong International Airport for your flight home. Depending on your departure time, you may have a chance for some last-minute souvenir shopping or a relaxing morning coffee with a view of the skyline before bidding farewell to China.
