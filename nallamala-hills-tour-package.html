  • Day 1

    Arrival at Srisailam – A Spiritual Start

    Your journey begins with a warm welcome at Hyderabad, where Travolex arranges your comfortable transfer to Srisailam – a scenic drive of approximately five hours that winds through the enchanting Nallamala forest. Along the way, you’ll stop to witness the serene beauty of Mallela Theertham Waterfalls, a picturesque cascade hidden amidst thick forest, known for its spiritual significance and tranquil ambiance. Upon reaching Srisailam, check in to your hotel or forest resort, relax for the evening, and enjoy a peaceful dinner surrounded by the natural charm of the hills. The day sets the tone for a spiritual and nature-infused retreat.

  • Day 2

    Exploring Srisailam – Temples, Caves & River Views

    Wake up to the misty mornings of Srisailam and begin your day with a hearty breakfast. The day is dedicated to exploring the spiritual and cultural treasures of the region. Visit the revered Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva and a powerful pilgrimage site deeply rooted in Indian mythology. After absorbing the temple’s divine atmosphere, head towards the scenic Pathala Ganga — the banks of the Krishna River accessed via ropeway or steps — offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and waters. If you're inclined, take a peaceful boat ride on the river. Later in the afternoon, venture into the mystical Akkamahadevi Caves, believed to have been the meditation site of the 12th-century saint. Return to your hotel by evening for a quiet dinner and stargazing session or a casual walk through the forest paths surrounding your stay.

  • Day 3

    Nature & Wildlife – Nallamala Forest Experience

    After breakfast, immerse yourself in the wild beauty of the Nallamala hills with a guided forest experience curated by Travolex. Begin your journey into the dense woods of the Nagarjunasagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, the largest in India, where chances of spotting rare wildlife such as sloth bears, leopards, or even the elusive tiger are part of the thrill. Visit the Farahabad Viewpoint, offering sweeping vistas of the thick green canopy and deep valleys. Enjoy a packed lunch or a traditional forest-side meal prepared with local ingredients. In the late afternoon, stop by a nearby tribal village to learn about the local way of life, their customs, and traditional crafts. As the sun sets behind the forested hills, return to your resort for a relaxing evening around a bonfire, sharing stories and enjoying the sounds of the forest at night.

  • Day 4

    Departure – Farewell to the Hills

    On your final day, savor a quiet morning amidst the peaceful wilderness. After breakfast, check out from the resort and begin your return journey to Hyderabad. If time permits, Travolex offers the option to stop at local handloom or spice markets along the way, where you can shop for authentic souvenirs. With hearts full of memories and lungs full of fresh mountain air, you’ll arrive back in the city by late afternoon, marking the end of a soulful and refreshing escape into the wild and spiritual heart of Andhra Pradesh.
