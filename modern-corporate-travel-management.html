    The New Rules of Business Travel

    Corporate Business travel has changed fundamentally. The rise of hybrid work, tighter budgets, global health concerns, and shifting employee expectations are forcing companies to rethink their approach. Key changes include:
    • Focus on purposeful travel: Companies are prioritizing trips with clear ROI—such as client meetings, strategic events, or team-building retreats.
    • Sustainability in travel decisions: Eco-conscious organizations are now measuring and minimizing their carbon footprint.
    • Increased compliance: There’s greater emphasis on travel policies, risk management, and real-time traveler tracking.
