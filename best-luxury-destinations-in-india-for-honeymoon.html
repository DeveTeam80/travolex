    Udaipur – The City of Lakes and Royal Romance

    Why go: Often dubbed “The Venice of the East,” Udaipur is the epitome of regal elegance. Set against the backdrop of the Aravalli Hills, this city of palaces and serene lakes is a dream for couples seeking a royal start to married life.

    Top luxury experiences:

    • Stay at the iconic Taj Lake Palace
    • floating in the middle of Lake Pichola
    • Private sunset boat rides on Lake Fatehsagar
    • Candlelit dinners in 18th-century havelis
    • Couple spa therapies with lake views

    Perfect for: Couples who love heritage, history, and royal experiences.

    Goa – Sun, Sand, and Sophistication

    Goa is not just about parties and beaches—it’s also home to some of India’s most luxurious resorts, tranquil beaches, and boutique experiences perfect for a honeymoon.

    Top luxury experiences

    :
    • Beachfront villas at The Leela Goa or Alila Diwa
    • Sunset cruises on private yachts
    • Private beach dinners with personal butlers
    • Ayurvedic couple massages in lush garden spas

    Perfect for: Couples looking for a mix of relaxation, fun, and tropical romance.

    Gulmarg – Kashmir’s Snowy Wonderland

    If your idea of romance involves snow-capped peaks, cozy fires, and scenic seclusion, then Gulmarg in Kashmir is your winter honeymoon paradise.

    Top luxury experiences:

    • Stay at the lavish Khyber Himalayan Resort & Spa
    • Gondola rides offering panoramic views of the Himalayas
    • Skiing, snowboarding, and guided snow treks
    • Hot cocoa by the fireplace in a snow-covered chalet

    Perfect for: Couples who love winter escapes and mountain magic.

    Alleppey – Romance on the Backwaters of Kerala

    Drift along the tranquil backwaters of Kerala on a private houseboat and experience a honeymoon that's as serene as it is intimate.

    Top luxury experiences

    :
    • Stay in a fully-serviced luxury houseboat with gourmet meals
    • Couple’s Ayurveda treatments at Kumarakom Lake Resort
    • Sunset cruises with candlelight dinners
    • Bird-watching and village walks through coconut groves

    Perfect for: Couples who want a slow, soulful, and scenic honeymoon.

    Jaipur – Regal Retreats and Cultural Charm

    The Pink City offers a majestic mix of opulence and history. With grand forts, luxury hotels, and immersive cultural experiences, Jaipur is perfect for a royal honeymoon affair.

    Top luxury experiences

    :
    • Stay like royalty at Rambagh Palace or Samode Haveli
    • Private elephant safaris and palace tours
    • Jewelry shopping and custom traditional attire
    • Rooftop dining with panoramic views of the city

    Perfect for: Couples who love rich culture, grand architecture, and timeless elegance.
