  • Day 1

    Arrival in Frankfurt – Gateway to Germany

    Arrive at Frankfurt International Airport, where you’ll be met by a Travolex representative. After hotel check-in, spend the rest of the day at leisure exploring this modern financial hub. Optionally, take a relaxed evening walk along the Main River or visit the Römer, Frankfurt’s historic town hall.

  • Day 2

    Frankfurt – Rhine Valley Excursion

    Embark on a scenic full-day tour to the Rhine Valley, famous for its vineyard-covered hills, medieval castles, and charming villages. Enjoy a Rhine River cruise, explore the picturesque town of Rüdesheim, and savor local wines during a vineyard visit.
  • Day 3

    Frankfurt to Heidelberg to Stuttgart

    Depart Frankfurt for Heidelberg, home to Germany’s oldest university. Visit the Heidelberg Castle, stroll along the Philosopher’s Walk, and soak in the baroque charm of the Old Town. In the afternoon, continue to Stuttgart, the city of cars and culture.
  • Day 4

    Stuttgart – Mercedes, Museums & More

    Begin your day with a visit to the Mercedes-Benz Museum, a must-see for car enthusiasts. Later, explore the Stuttgart Palace Square and the Kunstmuseum (Art Museum). In the evening, you may opt for a vineyard hike or a traditional Swabian dinner.
  • Day 5

    Stuttgart to Füssen – Neuschwanstein Castle

    Travel to Füssen, a charming Bavarian town at the foot of the Alps. Visit the magical Neuschwanstein Castle, the inspiration for Disney’s Sleeping Beauty castle. Enjoy panoramic views and a relaxed evening in the alpine surroundings.
  • Day 6

    Füssen to Munich – Bavarian Capital

    After breakfast, head to Munich, the vibrant capital of Bavaria. Upon arrival, explore Marienplatz, visit the Residenz Palace, and enjoy a guided tour of the BMW Museum or the Deutsches Museum. Don't miss trying an authentic Bavarian pretzel!
  • Day 7

    Munich – Day Trip to Dachau or Optional Excursions

    Choose between a deeply moving visit to the Dachau Concentration Camp Memorial Site for a powerful historical experience, a scenic day trip to Salzburg, Austria—Mozart’s enchanting birthplace—just 1.5 hours away, or a leisurely day in Munich exploring local shops, unwinding in the serene English Garden, and savoring Bavarian cuisine at your own pace.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Munich - Auf Wiedersehen, Germany!

    After breakfast, transfer to Munich Airport for your departure. Take with you unforgettable memories of castles, culture, and cobbled streets.
