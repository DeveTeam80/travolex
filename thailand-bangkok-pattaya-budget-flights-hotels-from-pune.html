Pune → Bangkok: Flight Basics

  • Airports: PNQ (Pune) usually connects via BOM/BLR/DEL to BKK (Suvarnabhumi) or DMK (Don Mueang).
  • Budget hacks: Early booking (6-8 weeks), mid-week travel (Tue-Thu), and red-eye connections reduce fares.
  • Baggage: Low-cost carriers may charge for check-in; compare total fare, not base price.

Where to Stay (Budget to Value)

  • Bangkok: Pratunam (shopping), Sukhumvit (nightlife & BTS access), Riverside (scenic; slightly pricier).
  • Pattaya: Central Pattaya (walkable), North Pattaya (quieter), Jomtien (family-friendly).
  • Ask Travolex for vegetarian/Jain-friendly hotels and near-metro stays.

5-Day Budget Itinerary (Sample)

  1. Day 1 - Arrive Bangkok: Airport pickup, check-in, Asiatique night market & riverfront dinner.
  2. Day 2 - Bangkok Essentials: Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Wat Arun (ferry), evening shopping at MBK/Platinum.
  3. Day 3 - Transfer to Pattaya: En-route Sriracha Tiger Zoo/Nong Nooch (optional), Alcazar/Tiffany’s show at night.
  4. Day 4 - Coral Island + Pattaya Highlights: Speedboat to Koh Larn, glass-bottom/parasailing (optional), Walking Street (if desired).
  5. Day 5 - Back to Bangkok & Fly Home: Outlet shopping or Safari World (if time) → airport transfer.

Visa, Forex & Connectivity

  • Visa: Visa on Arrival/e-visa options for Indians (subject to change). Travolex shares latest documents & fees.
  • Money: Carry a forex card + small THB cash. Many places accept cards/UPI-linked international rails where available.
  • SIM/Wi-Fi: Airport tourist SIMs are easy; we can pre-arrange eSIMs too.

Best Time to Visit

  • Nov-Mar: Pleasant weather; higher prices—book early.
  • Apr-Jun: Hot but value deals.
  • Jul-Oct: Monsoon; great hotel rates—keep indoor plans handy.

Savings & Safety Tips

  • Use BTS/MRT in Bangkok; pre-book airport & intercity transfers.
  • Pick combo tickets (island + water sports) to save more.
  • Respect temple dress codes; avoid unlicensed guides.
  • Street food is amazing—choose busy, clean stalls.
