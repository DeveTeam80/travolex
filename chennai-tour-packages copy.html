Chennai

The capital of Tamil Nadu, Chennai is a perfect blend of South Indian heritage, ancient temples, colonial charm, and a growing cosmopolitan vibe. With a Chennai tour package from Pune, dive into rich traditions, coastal drives, classical music, and spicy Chettinad cuisine.

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Chennai – City Welcome

    Your

    Chennai experience

    begins with your arrival at Chennai International Airport or railway station, where our representative will receive you and escort you to your hotel. After check-in, spend the afternoon relaxing. In the evening, head to Marina Beach, India’s longest urban beach, to enjoy the sea breeze, street food, and a beautiful sunset. If time permits, visit the nearby Santhome Cathedral Basilica, built over the tomb of St. Thomas the Apostle. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 2

    Chennai City Sightseeing

    On Day 2, after breakfast, enjoy a full day of

    sightseeing in Chennai

    . Begin with a visit to Kapaleeshwarar Temple, a beautiful example of Dravidian architecture, followed by the Government Museum and Fort St. George, which offers insights into the colonial past of the city. Later, stop by Vivekananda House, and end your day at Elliot’s Beach, a calmer alternative to Marina. Spend the evening at leisure or enjoy shopping at T. Nagar or Express Avenue Mall. Overnight stay in Chennai.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Mahabalipuram

    Day 3 is reserved for a

    heritage day trip to Mahabalipuram

    (approx. 60 km from Chennai), a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore the Shore Temple, Five Rathas, and Arjuna’s Penance, each carved intricately from stone. Enjoy coastal views and seafood by the beachside restaurants. Return to Chennai by evening. Overnight stay in Chennai.

  • Day 4

    Temple & Heritage Tour to Kanchipuram

    On Day 4, take a short excursion to Kanchipuram, known as the "City of Thousand Temples" and famous for its silk sarees. Visit iconic temples like Kailasanathar Temple and Ekambareswarar Temple, and explore a local silk weaving unit to witness the craftsmanship behind the famous Kanchipuram silk. Return to Chennai in the evening. Overnight stay in Chennai.

  • Day 5

    Departure

    On the final day, enjoy a relaxed breakfast at your hotel. If time allows before your departure, you can visit Guindy National Park or do some last-minute shopping. Later, check out and transfer to the airport or railway station with pleasant memories of your cultural and coastal Chennai trip.
