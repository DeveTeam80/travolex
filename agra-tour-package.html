  • Day 1

    Arrival in Agra – A City of Timeless Grandeur

    Your journey begins with your arrival in Agra, where a Travolex representative will receive you at the railway station or Delhi airport (if you opt for the transfer). As you travel towards the city, anticipation builds for one of the world’s most iconic destinations. After check-in at your chosen heritage hotel or modern stay, take some time to relax. In the late afternoon, ease into the city’s rhythm with a visit to Mehtab Bagh — the perfect spot to view the Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna River during sunset, away from the crowds. As twilight paints the sky, enjoy a traditional Mughlai dinner and settle in for the night.

  • Day 2

    Taj Mahal & Mughal Marvels

    Rise early for the most magical moment of your trip — a sunrise visit to the Taj Mahal. As the first light touches the white marble, witness the monument come alive in hues of gold and pink. A guided tour offers insights into its timeless love story, intricate architecture, and Mughal history. After breakfast back at the hotel, continue to explore the majestic Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage Site that was once the seat of the Mughal Empire. In the afternoon, visit the Tomb of Itimad-ud-Daulah, often referred to as the “Baby Taj” for its delicate craftsmanship and beautiful inlay work. End your day with an optional cultural show or walk through Agra’s old bazaars, known for marble artifacts, leather goods, and zardozi embroidery. Return to your hotel for a restful evening.

  • Day 3

    Day Excursion to Fatehpur Sikri & Local Culture

    After breakfast, head out for a day trip to Fatehpur Sikri, the abandoned Mughal capital located about an hour from Agra. Walk through its royal courtyards, palaces, and the grand Jama Masjid, all steeped in stories of Emperor Akbar’s reign. Don’t miss the towering Buland Darwaza and the peaceful tomb of Salim Chishti, believed to grant wishes. On the way back, stop for lunch at a local countryside retreat before returning to Agra. In the evening, immerse yourself in a local experience — visit Kalakriti Cultural Centre or participate in a hands-on workshop on marble inlay or miniature painting, offered as an optional add-on by Travolex. Enjoy your final night in Agra with a farewell dinner featuring local flavors.

  • Day 4

    Departure – Farewell to the Mughal Legacy

    On your last day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast and prepare to check out. Depending on your departure schedule, Travolex can arrange a short morning visit to the Akbar’s Tomb at Sikandra or a peaceful walk through Ram Bagh, the oldest Mughal garden in India. Afterward, you will be dropped at the railway station or Delhi airport as you bid goodbye to Agra — taking with you the lasting impressions of marble, history, and royal elegance.
