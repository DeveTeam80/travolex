  • Day 1

    Arrival in Amritsar – First Glimpse of the Golden City

    Your journey begins with your arrival at Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport or Amritsar Railway Station. After a warm Travolex welcome, you are transferred to your hotel for check-in and some rest. Later in the evening, head to the revered Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib) for the soul-stirring Palki Sahib ceremony, where the holy Guru Granth Sahib is carried in a golden palanquin amidst devotional chants. The temple, illuminated at night and reflected in the Amrit Sarovar (holy tank), is a sight of absolute peace and devotion. Return to the hotel for your overnight stay in Amritsar.

  • Day 2

    Amritsar Heritage Walk & Cultural Exploration

    After breakfast, begin your day with a guided Heritage Walk through the historic lanes of the old city. Explore hidden gems like Akhara Sangalwala, Qila Ahluwalia, and Chowk Jalebian, and learn about Amritsar’s fascinating Sikh and colonial history. Continue to the Partition Museum, an emotional yet informative space dedicated to the stories of 1947. Post lunch, visit Gobindgarh Fort, where history comes alive with live performances, museum exhibits, and the thrilling 6D Sher-e-Punjab show on Maharaja Ranjit Singh. Enjoy the cultural vibe of the fort with traditional food stalls and local artisans. Overnight stay in Amritsar.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Wagah Border – Patriotism at Its Peak

    After a relaxed morning, spend some time shopping for authentic Phulkari, Punjabi juttis, or Amritsari papad and wadiyan in the bustling Hall Bazaar or Katra Jaimal Singh Market. Later in the afternoon, drive to Wagah Border, about 30 km from the city. Arrive early to get a good spot for the flag-lowering ceremony, a dramatic display of military pageantry and national pride conducted by the Indian and Pakistani forces. The crowd’s energy and patriotic chants make this an unforgettable experience. After the ceremony, return to Amritsar for dinner and an overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Spiritual & Culinary Trails – Optional Rural Experience

    On this day, you may revisit the Golden Temple early in the morning to witness Langar (community kitchen) in full operation — a heartwarming example of selfless service and unity. Later, visit the historic Jallianwala Bagh, the site of the 1919 massacre, now a serene memorial that tells a powerful story. In the afternoon, Travolex offers an optional Punjabi Village Experience, where you can enjoy a traditional meal, tractor rides, turban-tying, folk dance performances, and even try your hand at farming — an immersive look into rural Punjab’s heartland. Return by evening and relax at your hotel. Overnight stay in Amritsar.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Amritsar – Farewell with Blessings

    After breakfast and a relaxed morning, check out from your hotel. You will be transferred to the airport or railway station for your onward journey, departing with not just souvenirs, but also memories of spiritual peace, patriotic pride, and the warm hospitality of Punjab.
