  • Day 1

    Arrival in Dublin – A Capital of Culture

    Arrive at Dublin Airport, where a Travolex representative will greet you and assist with your transfer to the hotel. Spend your afternoon exploring Dublin’s lively city centre — visit Grafton Street, stroll through St. Stephen’s Green, or enjoy a pint in Temple Bar. Optional evening: Traditional Irish music and dinner show.

  • Day 2

    Dublin – History, Literature & Guinness

    Start your day with a guided tour of Trinity College and the Book of Kells, followed by visits to Dublin Castle and St. Patrick’s Cathedral. In the afternoon, head to the iconic Guinness Storehouse for a self-guided tour ending with a pint in the Gravity Bar, offering panoramic city views.
  • Day 3

    Dublin to Kilkenny to Killarney

    Depart Dublin and journey to Kilkenny, a medieval city rich in history. Visit Kilkenny Castle and explore the cobblestone streets filled with artisan shops and pubs. Continue through the scenic Irish countryside to the charming town of Killarney, nestled at the edge of Killarney National Park.
  • Day 4

    Killarney – Ring of Kerry Tour

    Embark on a full-day guided tour of the Ring of Kerry, one of Ireland’s most stunning drives. Discover rugged coastlines, quaint villages, and panoramic views of mountains, lakes, and the Atlantic Ocean. Stop at Muckross House and Gardens, and enjoy plenty of photo opportunities throughout the day.
  • Day 5

    Killarney to Dingle Peninsula

    Take a day trip to the Dingle Peninsula, famous for its dramatic cliffs, Gaelic culture, and ocean views. Visit Slea Head Drive, Dingle Town, and perhaps catch a glimpse of Fungie the Dolphin. Enjoy fresh seafood in a coastal pub before returning to Killarney.
  • Day 6

    Killarney to Cliffs of Moher to Galway

    Travel north to the spectacular Cliffs of Moher, rising over 700 feet above the Atlantic. Walk along the cliffside trails and enjoy breathtaking views of the wild Irish coast. Continue via the Burren, a unique limestone landscape, before arriving in Galway, Ireland’s artsy and bohemian city.
  • Day 7

    Galway – Connemara or Aran Islands (Optional)

    Today is yours to explore. Choose a guided excursion to the Connemara region, known for its mountains, lakes, and traditional Irish culture. Alternatively, take a ferry to the Aran Islands, stepping back in time to a place of ancient forts and Irish-speaking locals.
  • Day 8

    Return to Dublin – Departure - Slán Abhaile

    After breakfast, travel back to Dublin for your departure flight. Optional: Depending on flight time, you may stop en route at Clonmacnoise Monastic Site or enjoy some last-minute shopping at Kildare Village Outlet.
