  • Day 1

    Arrival in Guwahati - Temples & River Serenity

    Arrive in Guwahati and visit the holy Kamakhya Temple before enjoying a sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

  • Day 2

    Guwahati to Kaziranga - Into the Wild

    Travel to Kaziranga National Park, home to the world-famous one-horned rhinoceros. Check in to your eco-resort and experience a local Bihu dance performance in the evening.
  • Day 3

    Jungle Safaris & Tea Trails

    Enjoy an early morning jeep safari through Kaziranga’s wildlife zones. Visit a nearby tea estate and the Kaziranga Orchid Park for a glimpse into Assam’s natural and cultural beauty.
  • Day 4

    Day Trip to Majuli Island (Optional) or Explore Local Culture

    Take a day trip to Majuli, the world’s largest river island, and explore its Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries) and Mishing tribal villages. Alternatively, enjoy another safari or tea garden visit near Kaziranga.
  • Day 5

    Kaziranga to Guwahati - Culture & Markets

    Drive back to Guwahati and visit Umananda Temple on Peacock Island. Shop at Fancy Bazaar for Assam tea, silk, and bamboo crafts.
  • Day 6

    Departure from Guwahati - Bidding Adieu to Assam

    After breakfast, check out and depart with memories of Assam’s wildlife, culture, and serene landscapes.

The ideal time to visit Assam is between October and April, when the weather is pleasant and Kaziranga National Park is open for safaris. The spring and winter months also offer clear skies for tea estate visits, river cruises, and cultural festivals like Bihu.
