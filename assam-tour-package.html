  • Day 1

    Arrival in Guwahati – Temples & River Serenity

    Upon your arrival at Guwahati Airport or Railway Station, a Travolex representative will receive you and assist with your hotel transfer. After checking in and settling down, begin your journey with a visit to the ancient Kamakhya Temple, a revered Shakti Peetha perched atop Nilachal Hill. Spend some peaceful time exploring the temple complex and enjoying panoramic views of the Brahmaputra River. Later in the evening, enjoy a sunset river cruise on the Brahmaputra — a soothing introduction to Assam’s natural charm. Return to your hotel for dinner and your first overnight stay in Guwahati.

  • Day 2

    Guwahati to Kaziranga – Into the Wild

    >After an early breakfast, check out and drive to Kaziranga National Park (approx. 5–6 hours), known worldwide for its population of the Great Indian One-Horned Rhinoceros. Upon arrival, check into your jungle lodge or eco-resort surrounded by the verdant landscape of the Eastern Himalayas. Spend your afternoon at leisure or walk to a nearby tribal village to observe traditional Assamese life. In the evening, enjoy a folk dance performance (Bihu or Jhumur) at your resort. Dinner and overnight stay in Kaziranga.

  • Day 3

    Jungle Safaris & Tea Trails

    Start your day early with a thrilling jeep safari through Kaziranga's central or western range. As the mist lifts off the tall elephant grass, spot rhinos, elephants, wild buffalo, swamp deer, and possibly even a tiger. After breakfast at the lodge, take some time to relax. In the afternoon, explore a nearby tea estate, walk through emerald green plantations, and learn about Assam’s globally renowned tea. Later, you may also visit the Kaziranga Orchid and Biodiversity Park, a conservation center that showcases over 500 varieties of orchids, traditional houses, and folk instruments. Return to your resort for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Day Trip to Majuli Island (Optional) or Explore Local Culture

    Today, Travolex offers you two options. You can either opt for a full-day excursion to Majuli Island, the world’s largest river island located on the Brahmaputra. A scenic drive followed by a ferry ride brings you to this peaceful, offbeat gem. Visit Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries) like Auniati or Kamalabari, and witness the simple and spiritual lifestyle of the monks and local Mishing tribes. Alternatively, if you prefer to stay around Kaziranga, you can enjoy a second jeep safari in a different zone of the park or explore more tea gardens and local tribal villages. Return to Kaziranga or Jorhat by evening, depending on your travel route. Overnight stay.

  • Day 5

    Kaziranga to Guwahati – Culture & Markets

    After breakfast, check out and drive back to Guwahati. On the way, enjoy the scenic countryside filled with paddy fields and tea estates. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and freshen up. Later, visit the Umananda Temple, situated on Peacock Island in the middle of the Brahmaputra River. You can reach the island by a short ferry ride. In the evening, explore Fancy Bazaar or Paltan Bazaar, ideal for picking up local silk, bamboo crafts, and authentic Assam tea. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

  • Day 6

    Departure from Guwahati – Bidding Adieu to Assam

    After a leisurely breakfast, check out from your hotel. A Travolex representative will assist you with your transfer to Guwahati Airport or Railway Station. You leave with unforgettable memories of Assam’s wildlife, temples, tea gardens, river islands, and unmatched serenity.
