When to Visit London

The best time to visit Paris is typically during spring (April to June) or autumn (September to October), when the weather is mild, crowds are manageable, and the city is especially picturesque. In spring, blooming gardens like Jardin du Luxembourg and sidewalk cafés come alive, making it perfect for romantic strolls. Summer (July to August) offers long sunny days and plenty of events like Paris Plages and Bastille Day, but also brings heavy tourist crowds and higher prices. For a quieter, more local experience, autumn showcases golden leaves along the Seine and cultural festivals across the city. Winter (November to February) is the least crowded and most affordable time to visit, with charming holiday lights, fewer lines at museums, and the magic of Christmas markets—ideal for travelers who don't mind cooler temperatures.
