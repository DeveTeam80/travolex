  • Day 1

    Arrival in Gangtok – Welcome to the Capital of Sikkim

    Arrive at Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station and enjoy a scenic drive to Gangtok through forests, waterfalls, and mountain roads. Check in to your hotel and relax. In the evening, stroll along MG Marg—Sikkim’s iconic, no-vehicle walkway filled with cafés, shops, and views. Overnight stay in Gangtok. `

  • Day 2

    Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

    Start early for a full-day visit to the glacial Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft), famous for its crystal waters and dramatic mountain backdrop. Continue to Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a revered shrine honoring an Indian soldier. If weather and permits allow, visit Nathula Pass (14,140 ft) on the Indo-China border. Return to Gangtok by evening. Overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Gangtok Local Sightseeing – Monasteries & Viewpoints

    After breakfast, visit Rumtek Monastery, a major Tibetan Buddhist center. Continue to the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology, Do Drul Chorten, and the Flower Exhibition Centre with its beautiful orchids. End your tour at Tashi View Point for stunning Kanchenjunga panoramas (weather permitting). Return to hotel and relax.

  • Day 4

    Leisure Day – Optional Adventure or Local Exploration

    Spend the day at leisure. Choose from optional visits such as Banjhakri Waterfalls, Gangtok Ropeway, paragliding, or rafting on the Teesta River. Explore local markets, enjoy hill cafés, or unwind at your hotel. Overnight in Gangtok.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Gangtok

    After breakfast, check out and drive back to Bagdogra or NJP for your departure. Leave with memories of snowy peaks, spiritual sites, and the serene charm of Sikkim.
