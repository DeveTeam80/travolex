  • Day 1

    Arrival in Gangtok – Welcome to the Capital of Sikkim

    Your journey begins with a scenic drive from Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station to Gangtok, the charming capital of Sikkim. As you ascend the winding mountain roads, mist-covered forests and gushing streams set the tone for a serene retreat. Upon arrival in Gangtok, check into your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. In the evening, take a stroll along MG Marg, a pedestrian-friendly promenade lined with cafes, shops, and views of the surrounding hills. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

  • Day 2

    Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

    After an early breakfast, embark on a full-day excursion to Tsomgo Lake, a glacial lake located at an altitude of 12,400 ft and surrounded by rugged mountains. The lake remains frozen in winter and reflects the clear blue sky in summer, making it a photographer’s delight. Continue your journey to the revered Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a shrine built in memory of an Indian army soldier. Subject to permit and weather conditions, you may also visit Nathula Pass, the historic Indo-China border crossing at 14,140 ft. After a thrilling day in the high Himalayas, return to Gangtok by evening for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Gangtok Local Sightseeing – Monasteries & Viewpoints

    The third day is dedicated to exploring Gangtok’s cultural and natural attractions. After breakfast, begin with a visit to the iconic Rumtek Monastery, one of the most significant Buddhist monasteries in Sikkim, known for its Tibetan architecture and peaceful aura. Next, visit the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology, home to a rich collection of Buddhist scriptures and artifacts. Continue to Do Drul Chorten, a stupa built in 1945, followed by a walk through the Flower Exhibition Centre showcasing exotic Himalayan blooms. End your sightseeing at Tashi View Point, offering spectacular views of the Kanchenjunga range on a clear day. Return to the hotel and relax. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

  • Day 4

    Leisure Day – Optional Adventure or Local Exploration

    This day is kept free for guests to enjoy Gangtok at their own pace. You may choose to explore local markets, sip tea in hillside cafés, or take an optional visit to Banjhakri Waterfalls, known for its landscaped gardens and cascading waters. Adventure lovers can opt for paragliding, river rafting in the Teesta River, or a cable car ride for panoramic views of the city. Alternatively, relax in your hotel and enjoy the quiet mountain atmosphere. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Gangtok

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and begin your drive back to Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station for your onward journey. As you descend from the hills, carry with you memories of misty mornings, sacred shrines, mountain adventures, and the serene soul of Sikkim.
