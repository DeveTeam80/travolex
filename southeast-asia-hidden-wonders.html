1. Luang Prabang, Laos


Luang Prabang, a UNESCO World Heritage site where spirituality and old world elegance collide, is hidden away in the lush landscape of Laos. This city, renowned for its calm temples, regal mansions, and French colonial architecture, provides a calm diversion from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
Highlights include touring the night markets, going to the Kuang Si Waterfalls, and participating in the daybreak almsgiving ceremony. Mekong river cruises, guided temple visits, and cozy lodgings with a touch of the local way of life are all included in the carefully planned excursions that Travolex provides.



2. Bai Tu Long Bay, Vietnam


Bai Tu Long Bay provides a more calm, private experience, whereas Ha Long Bay frequently garners all the attention. Magnificent limestone cliffs, undiscovered caverns, and pristine beaches perfect for swimming or kayaking can all be found around the bay.
With scheduled cruises that transport you across beautiful emerald seas, island excursions, and delicious meals served onboard, Travolex guarantees an opulent experience. With this hidden jewel, you may escape the masses and experience the enchantment of unspoiled nature.



3. Borneo, Malaysia


Borneo is a haven for those who enjoy the outdoors and one of the few locations on Earth where you can see endangered species like pygmy elephants and orangutans. See unique birds and creatures in their native habitat by going on a river safari along the Kinabatangan River, trekking through the Sabah rainforests, or climbing Mount Kinabalu.
Packages from Travolex include guided excursions across these varied ecosystems, together with cozy accommodations, transportation, and activities catered to the interests of families and thrill-seekers alike.



4. Si Phan Don (Four Thousand Islands), Laos

A peaceful island beside the Mekong River, Si Phan Don gives guests an opportunity to decompress and get in touch with the natural world. Explore waterfalls like Li Phi Falls, go kayaking across the islands, or take a relaxed bike ride through the villages of Don Det and Don Khon.
Travolex makes sure your trip is as effortless as the river's flow by providing scheduled boat trips, cozy homestays, and chances to see the endangered Irrawaddy dolphins.
