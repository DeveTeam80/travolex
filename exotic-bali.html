  • Day 1

    Arrival in Bali - Welcome to Paradise

    Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport (Denpasar, Bali). Meet our representative and transfer to your luxury hotel. Complete check-in and relax. Enjoy the rest of the day exploring local markets, beaches, or indulge in Balinese spa treatments.

  • Day 2

    Full-Day Kintamani Volcano & Ubud Tour

    Begin your Bali sightseeing tour with a visit to Batubulan Village to witness the iconic Barong Dance. Explore Celuk Village for stunning silver and gold crafts, and marvel at the scenic Tegalalang Rice Terraces. Continue to Kintamani Volcano (Mount Batur) and enjoy lunch overlooking the crater and lake. Visit Tirta Empul Temple for a spiritual experience, then sample Bali’s famous Luwak Coffee at a local plantation.

  • Day 3

    Benoa Beach Adventure & Uluwatu Temple Sunset Tour

    Start your day at Benoa Beach, the hotspot for Bali water sports – enjoy jet skiing, parasailing, and banana boat rides. After lunch, visit the iconic Uluwatu Temple, perched dramatically on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean. Witness the mesmerizing Kecak Fire Dance as the sun sets.

  • Day 4

    Nusa Penida Island Tour

    Sail to Nusa Penida Island by ferry. Discover the natural wonders of Angel’s Billabong, Broken Beach, and the world-famous Kelingking Beach with its T-Rex-shaped cliff. Enjoy lunch and relax at Crystal Bay, known for its snorkeling and crystal-clear waters. Return to Bali by evening.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Bali

    After breakfast, check out from your hotel. Transfer to Bali International Airport for your onward journey, taking unforgettable Bali memories home.
