  • Day 1

    Arrival in Athens – Cradle of Western Civilization

    Arrive at Athens International Airport, where a Travolex representative will assist with your transfer to the hotel. Settle in and enjoy your first taste of the city. Take a relaxed stroll through Plaka, the old historical neighborhood of Athens, or enjoy a sunset view from Lycabettus Hill.

  • Day 2

    Athens – Acropolis and Ancient Landmarks

    Embark on a guided tour of Athens’ most iconic sites, including the Acropolis, Parthenon, Temple of Olympian Zeus, and the Ancient Agora. Visit the Acropolis Museum for a deep dive into classical art and architecture. Enjoy an evening at leisure, perhaps dining at a rooftop taverna with views of the illuminated Parthenon.
  • Day 3

    Athens to Mykonos – Island Life Begins

    After breakfast, transfer to the port and board a ferry to Mykonos, the cosmopolitan island known for its windmills, whitewashed houses, and vibrant nightlife. Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel. In the evening, explore Mykonos Town (Chora), enjoy shopping in the narrow lanes, and dine by the sea at Little Venice.
  • Day 4

    Mykonos – Beaches and Ancient Delos (Optional)

    Spend your day enjoying Mykonos’ famous beaches like Psarou, Ornos, or Paradise Beach. For history lovers, take an optional half-day boat trip to the island of Delos, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the mythological birthplace of Apollo and Artemis.
  • Day 5

    Mykonos to Santorini – Postcard Perfection

    Transfer by high-speed ferry to Santorini, one of the most romantic destinations in the world. After check-in, spend your afternoon exploring Fira, the island’s capital, with its cliffside cafes and shops. Enjoy a magical sunset from the village of Oia, renowned for its spectacular views.
  • Day 6

    Santorini – Caldera Cruise and Volcanic Wonders

    Today, embark on a half-day Caldera cruise, sailing to the volcanic islands of Nea Kameni and Palea Kameni, where you can walk on volcanic craters and take a dip in the hot springs. Optional upgrade: Choose a sunset cruise with onboard dinner. Spend your evening at leisure, perhaps indulging in some local wine at a cliffside bar.
  • Day 7

    Santorini – Free Day to Unwind

    Enjoy a full day to relax or explore at your own pace. Visit the black sand beach of Kamari, discover Ancient Akrotiri, or enjoy wine tasting at a local Santorini vineyard. It’s your chance to soak up the beauty and serenity of this iconic island.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Santorini or Athens

    After breakfast, transfer to Santorini Airport for your international flight or domestic connection back to Athens (if applicable). Depart with unforgettable memories of myth, sun, sea, and stunning sunsets.
