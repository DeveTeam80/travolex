Best International Destinations for Winter



As winter rolls in, Indian travellers start dreaming of snow-capped mountains, tropical beaches, and cultural adventures beyond borders. Whether you’re seeking a white Christmas, a sunny escape, or a romantic winter honeymoon, Travolex brings you the ultimate list of best international destinations for winter 2025-26 - curated for every kind of traveller.



1. Switzerland - The Classic Winter Wonderland


Best for: Honeymooners, Luxury seekers, Snow lovers

There’s no place like Switzerland Travel Package in winter. Imagine cozying up in wooden chalets, skiing in Zermatt, and sipping hot chocolate by Lake Geneva. From the Swiss Alps to Lucerne’s Christmas markets, this fairy-tale destination is pure magic.

Top experiences:

  • Scenic train rides on the Glacier Express
  • Skiing in Interlaken and Jungfrau
  • Exploring Zurich’s winter festivals
Visa: Schengen visa required
Ideal travel months: December to March


2. Thailand - A Warm Winter Getaway


Best for: Budget travellers, Beach lovers, Families

For those who prefer sunshine to snowfall, Thailand is the perfect winter escape. Warm weather, tropical islands, and a vibrant nightlife make it a top pick for Indians looking for affordable international holidays.

Top experiences:

  • Beach hopping in Phuket and Krabi
  • Full Moon Party in Koh Phangan
  • Shopping and street food in Bangkok
Visa: Visa on arrival for Indians
Ideal travel months: November to February


3. Japan - Winter Festivals & Snow Adventures


Best for: Culture enthusiasts, Adventure seekers

Japan’s winter season is a spectacle - from Hokkaido’s snow festivals to onsen (hot spring) experiences. Witness Tokyo’s winter illuminations, ski in Niseko, and savor warm bowls of ramen after a day in the snow.

Top experiences:

  • Sapporo Snow Festival (February)
  • Visiting Mount Fuji in clear winter skies
  • Relaxing in hot springs at Hakone
Visa: Tourist visa required
Ideal travel months: December to February


4. Dubai - Luxury & Adventure in the Desert


Best for: Families, Shoppers, Luxury travellers

Just a short flight away, the Dubai travel package offers a perfect blend of sunshine, adventure, and festive celebrations. With pleasant weather and world-class attractions, it’s one of the top winter destinations for Indian travellers.

Top experiences:

  • Desert safari & dune bashing
  • Shopping at Dubai Shopping Festival (Jan–Feb)
  • Visiting the Burj Khalifa & Dubai Miracle Garden
Visa: Tourist e-visa for Indians
Ideal travel months: November to February


5. Maldives - Romantic Island Bliss


Best for: Couples, Honeymooners, Relaxation seekers

Craving turquoise lagoons and overwater villas? The Maldives is your paradise. Just a few hours from India, it’s an unbeatable destination for a peaceful and private winter escape.

Top experiences:

  • Scuba diving & snorkeling
  • Candlelight dinners by the sea
  • Spa retreats in luxury resorts
Visa: Visa on arrival for Indians
Ideal travel months: November to April


6. Turkey - Europe Meets Asia


Best for: History buffs, Food lovers, Explorers

With fewer crowds and cooler weather, Turkey is a dream destination in winter. Explore Istanbul’s ancient architecture, enjoy Cappadocia’s hot air balloons, and indulge in Turkish kebabs and baklava.

Top experiences:

  • Hot air balloon ride in Cappadocia
  • Exploring Hagia Sophia & Blue Mosque
  • Thermal pools of Pamukkale
Visa: E-visa available for Indians
Ideal travel months: December to March


7. Bali, Indonesia – The Tropical Dream


Best for: Wellness travellers, Families, Couples

Bali’s lush greenery, beach resorts, and spiritual vibes make it an evergreen choice for Indian tourists. Winter in Bali means fewer rains, pleasant temperatures, and calm beaches - perfect for relaxation or adventure.

Top experiences:

  • Sunrise trek at Mount Batur
  • Temple tours in Ubud
  • Water sports in Nusa Dua
Visa: Visa on arrival for Indians
Ideal travel months: May to October (but great even in December–Feb for warm weather)


Tips for Planning Your Winter 2025-26 Trip



  • Book early: Winter is the peak travel season for Indians. Secure flights and hotels in advance.

  • Check visa updates: Some countries offer e-visa or visa-free entry for Indians - always verify before booking.

  • Pack smart: Research local weather; winter in Tokyo is nothing like winter in Phuket!

  • Travel insurance: Always essential for international trips.
