  • Day 1

    Arrival in Colombo - Transfer to Kandy

    Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your Travolex representative. En route to Kandy, stop at the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage to watch elephants in a natural setting. In the evening, visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and enjoy a traditional Kandyan dance show. Overnight stay in Kandy.

  • Day 2

    Kandy to Nuwara Eliya - Little England of Sri Lanka

    After breakfast, visit the Royal Botanical Garden in Peradeniya. Then drive through the scenic hill country to Nuwara Eliya, known as “Little England.” Visit a tea factory, Ramboda Falls, Gregory Lake, and Victoria Park. Stroll through the charming colonial streets before resting overnight in Nuwara Eliya.
  • Day 3

    Nuwara Eliya - Ella - Yala

    Take the world-famous train ride from Nanu Oya to Ella, one of the most scenic journeys in Asia. Explore Nine Arches Bridge, Ella Gap, and Ravana Falls. Continue to Yala National Park, home to Sri Lanka’s largest leopard population. Overnight stay near Yala.
  • Day 4

    Yala Safari - Bentota Beach

    Start early with a jeep safari in Yala National Park to spot leopards, elephants, and exotic birds. Later, drive to Bentota, the beautiful beach destination on Sri Lanka’s southwest coast. Check in at your beachfront resort and relax by the Indian Ocean. Overnight stay in Bentota.
  • Day 5

    Bentota - Colombo

    After breakfast, enjoy a boat safari on the Madu River, explore mangroves and visit a sea turtle hatchery. Optional water sports like jet skiing and banana boat rides are available. In the afternoon, proceed to Colombo for a city tour including Galle Face Green, Independence Square, Gangaramaya Temple, and shopping at ODEL. Overnight stay in Colombo.
  • Day 6

    Departure from Colombo

    Enjoy breakfast and some free time for shopping or leisure before being transferred to Bandaranaike International Airport for your return flight. Depart with unforgettable memories of your Sri Lanka tour package.
