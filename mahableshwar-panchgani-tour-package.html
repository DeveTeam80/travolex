  • Day 1

    Arrival in Mahabaleshwar – Into the Western Ghats

    Your getaway begins with a scenic drive from Pune or Mumbai, arranged by Travolex. The journey winds through the lush Sahyadri ranges, gradually ascending into the cool, mist-laden air of Mahabaleshwar. En route, stop at Mapro Garden for a refreshing break with fresh strawberry treats, sandwiches, and garden views. Upon arrival, check in to your hill resort or colonial-style hotel, offering panoramic valley or forest views. Spend your first evening unwinding—take a stroll around the Venna Lake, enjoy a boat ride, or simply watch the sunset from Wilson Point, the highest spot in Mahabaleshwar. Wrap up the day with a warm dinner and cozy overnight stay.

  • Day 2

    Mahabaleshwar Sightseeing – Points, Temples & Forest Trails

    After a wholesome breakfast, set out to explore Mahabaleshwar’s charming attractions. Begin with a visit to the historic Mahabaleshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, followed by a walk through the sacred Panchganga Temple, where five rivers are believed to converge. Continue to popular viewpoints like Arthur’s Seat, Kate’s Point, and Elephant’s Head Point, each offering spectacular views of the deep valleys and dense forests. In the afternoon, explore the lush Lingmala Waterfall or take a forest trail for a quiet escape into nature. Enjoy a relaxed evening at the local market, where you can shop for handicrafts, honey, and famous Mahabaleshwar strawberries. Return to your resort for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Panchgani – Table Land & Strawberry Fields

    Today is reserved for exploring the nearby twin hill town of Panchgani, just a short drive away. After breakfast, head out to discover this quaint retreat known for its British-era charm, boarding schools, and sprawling table lands. Begin with a visit to Table Land—the second longest mountain plateau in Asia—offering surreal views and optional horse rides. Next, enjoy a walk through strawberry farms, where you can pick fresh berries during the season and sample homemade jams and syrups. Stop by Parsi Point and Sydney Point for postcard-worthy views of the Krishna River valley. After a scenic lunch at a café overlooking the hills, you may visit Devrai Art Village to explore tribal and eco-art creations. Return to Mahabaleshwar by evening for your final night’s stay, with an optional bonfire or candlelit dinner.

  • Day 4

    Departure – Farewell to the Hills

    After a leisurely breakfast, it’s time to check out and begin your return journey to Pune or Mumbai. En route, Travolex can arrange an optional stop at Wai to explore its ancient temples and river ghats, or at a strawberry processing unit to learn about local produce. Arrive back in the city by late afternoon with bags full of local goodies and hearts full of misty mountain memories.
