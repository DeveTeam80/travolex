Dubai, UAE


  • Why It’s Perfect: Known for its modern skyscrapers, luxury shopping, and bustling business scene, Dubai is a prime spot for corporate travelers. During Christmas, Dubai’s unique blend of culture and innovation offers a festive, cosmopolitan vibe.
  • Corporate Essentials: Dubai has world-renowned business hotels like the Burj Al Arab and meeting venues like the

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia


  • Why It’s Perfect: Kuala Lumpur is known for its affordability, cultural diversity, and modern business facilities. With warm weather and plenty of holiday-themed events, it’s a great spot for an

    affordable business trip

    around Christmas.
  • Corporate Essentials: Kuala Lumpur has many affordable hotels, such as budget-friendly five-star options in the city center, making it easy to find a comfortable yet economical stay. Travolex can arrange meetings, conference rooms, and transportation to ensure your business needs are met.
  • Festive Activities: Kuala Lumpur hosts various Christmas events, especially in its famous shopping malls like Pavilion KL and Suria KLCC, which feature festive displays and holiday sales. You can also explore street food markets and affordable local eateries after work.

Manila, Philippines

  • Why It’s Perfect: Manila is one of Southeast Asia’s most budget-friendly destinations, especially during the Christmas season, when the city is lively and decorated.
  • Corporate Essentials: Manila offers affordable hotels with meeting facilities, and co-working spaces are plentiful in business districts like Makati.
  • Festive Activities: Experience the Philippines’ famed Christmas parols (lanterns) and lively street festivities. Christmas lights displays and local holiday markets offer great places to relax after work.

Colombo, Sri Lanka

  • Why It’s Perfect: For those seeking a warm destination with affordable prices, Colombo provides a blend of business facilities and holiday charm.

  • Corporate Essentials: Many Colombo hotels offer cost-effective meeting rooms and business services, with Travolex arranging seamless bookings

  • Festive Activities: Colombo’s holiday decor is in full swing during December. Explore the Pettah Floating Market or take a sunset walk along Galle Face Green.
