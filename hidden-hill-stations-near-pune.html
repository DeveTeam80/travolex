Hidden Hill Stations Near Pune for a Perfect Long Weekend Getaway

28 October

Looking for a peaceful escape from Pune’s chaos? Skip the usual Lonavala and Mahabaleshwar routes - here are three hidden hill stations near Pune that are perfect for a relaxing long weekend surrounded by nature.


1. Bhandardara - The Serene Lakeside Retreat


Nestled in Ahmednagar district, Bhandardara is a calm, lesser-known hill station about 170 km from Pune.

Don't Miss: Famous for Arthur Lake, Randha Falls, and the Wilson Dam. Ideal for boating, hiking, and stargazing. Best visited during monsoon or winter for lush scenery.
Travel Tip: The drive takes around 4 hours; book a lake-view stay for the best experience.



2. Jawhar - The Hidden Cultural Gem


Around 250 km from Pune, Jawhar blends greenery, waterfalls, and tribal heritage.

Don't Miss: Visit Dabhosa Waterfall, Jai Vilas Palace, and explore Warli art. Quiet, scenic, and perfect for offbeat travelers who love rustic charm.
Travel Tip: Fewer luxury stays here, but that’s what keeps it peaceful and authentic.



3. Tamhini Ghat - The Monsoon Paradise


Just 60 km from Pune, Tamhini Ghat offers breathtaking valley views and countless waterfalls.

Don't Miss: Great for a short drive, nature walk, or photography session. Visit Mulshi Lake nearby for calm lakeside stays.
Travel Tip: Monsoon is magical, but drive carefully - roads can get slippery.



Why Choose These Hill Stations?


  • Closer to Pune - less travel, more relaxation.
  • Crowd-free - ideal for couples, families, and solo travelers.
  • Naturally beautiful - waterfalls, hills, and peaceful vibes all weekend long.
