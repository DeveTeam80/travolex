  • Day 1

    Arrival in Nainital – Welcome to the Lake District

    Your journey begins with a scenic drive to Nainital from Kathgodam Railway Station or Pantnagar Airport. As you ascend into the Kumaon hills, the air grows crisper and the views more picturesque. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and unwind after the journey. Later in the day, enjoy a peaceful walk along Mall Road, followed by a visit to Naina Devi Temple, perched beside the shimmering Naini Lake. In the evening, you can enjoy a boat ride on Naini Lake, watching the sunset reflect over the calm waters. Overnight stay in Nainital.

  • Day 2

    Nainital Local Sightseeing – Views, Rides & Trails

    After breakfast, set out to explore the many charms of Nainital. The day begins with a visit to Snow View Point, accessible by a thrilling cable car ride, offering panoramic views of the snow-clad Himalayan peaks. Continue to Tiffin Top, a scenic viewpoint ideal for a short hike or horseback ride. Stop by The Zoo, home to high-altitude wildlife like the Himalayan bear and snow leopard. Later, visit the Governor’s House (Raj Bhawan), a stunning example of British colonial architecture. Enjoy some shopping in Tibetan Market or grab a coffee at a lakeside café before returning to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Excursion to Bhimtal – Sattal – Naukuchiatal

    On Day 3, head out for a full-day excursion to the nearby lake towns. Your first stop is Bhimtal, known for its large, serene lake with a tiny island in the center. Continue to Sattal, a cluster of seven interconnected freshwater lakes surrounded by lush forests and migratory birds – a paradise for photographers and birdwatchers. The final stop is Naukuchiatal, a peaceful nine-cornered lake, perfect for boating, paragliding, or simply relaxing by the water. After a refreshing day amidst nature, return to Nainital for dinner and an overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Day Trip to Mukteshwar – Temples & Himalayan Views

    Begin your day with a drive to Mukteshwar, a quiet hill station located around 50 km from Nainital. En route, enjoy breathtaking views of the valleys and apple orchards. In Mukteshwar, visit the Mukteshwar Dham Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva and perched atop a hill with sweeping views of the Himalayas. For thrill-seekers, Chauli ki Jali, a cliff-side viewpoint, offers excellent spots for rappelling and photography. Explore local markets or enjoy a picnic lunch amid pine forests. In the late afternoon, return to Nainital to relax and reflect on your day. Overnight stay in Nainital.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Nainital

    After breakfast, check out from your hotel and prepare for your return journey to Kathgodam or Pantnagar. As you drive back through the winding forest roads, take with you the fresh mountain air, scenic memories, and the tranquil spirit of the Kumaon hills.
