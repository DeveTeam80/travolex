  • Day 1

    Arrival in Srinagar - Dal Lake & Mughal Gardens

    Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel or houseboat. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens-Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. In the evening, enjoy a relaxing Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

  • Day 2

    Srinagar to Gulmarg - Meadows & Gondola Ride

    Drive to Gulmarg, known for its lush meadows and winter snow adventures. Experience the world-famous Gulmarg Gondola (Asia’s highest cable car). Enjoy optional skiing or snow activities (seasonal). Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Srinagar to Pahalgam - Valley of Shepherds

    Travel to Pahalgam, stopping at the ancient Avantipur Ruins en route. Explore Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, or Chandanwari (optional via local taxi). Enjoy nature walks, horse riding, or valley views. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

  • Day 4

    Pahalgam to Srinagar - Local Crafts & Leisure

    Return to Srinagar. Visit local markets known for pashmina shawls, carpets, saffron, and walnut woodcraft. Optional visits include Hazratbal Shrine or Jamia Masjid. Try authentic Kashmiri cuisine in the evening. Overnight in Srinagar.

  • Day 5

    Departure from Srinagar

    After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your return journey with unforgettable Kashmir memories.
