  • Day 1

    Arrival in Tokyo

    Arrive at Tokyo Haneda or Narita Airport and transfer to your hotel. Evening free to explore Shinjuku’s neon nightlife or relax after your flight.

  • Day 2

    Tokyo City Tour

    Discover Asakusa’s Senso-ji Temple, Imperial Palace, Meiji Shrine, and trendy Harajuku. End your day at Tokyo Tower or the stunning teamLab Planets digital museum.
  • Day 3

    Mt. Fuji & Hakone Excursion

    Enjoy breathtaking views from Mt. Fuji’s 5th Station, then cruise on Lake Ashi and ride the Hakone Ropeway for views of Owakudani Valley. Optional stay in a traditional ryokan with an onsen.
  • Day 4

    Tokyo to Kyoto via Shinkansen

    Board Japan’s iconic bullet train to Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan. Visit Gion District or Nishiki Market, and enjoy an optional tea ceremony experience.
  • Day 5

    Kyoto Historical Tour

    Explore Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Ryoan-ji, and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, known for its 1,000 red torii gates.
  • Day 6

    Nara Day Trip

    Take a half-day trip to Nara to see Todai-ji Temple, Nara Park’s friendly deer, and Kasuga Taisha Shrine. Return to Kyoto in the evening.
  • Day 7

    Kyoto to Osaka - Urban Energy Meets Culinary Delights

    Travel to Osaka, Japan’s culinary capital. Visit Osaka Castle, the Umeda Sky Building, and lively Dotonbori Street for food and nightlife.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Osaka

    After breakfast, transfer to Kansai International Airport. Optional last-minute shopping in Shinsaibashi before your flight home.
