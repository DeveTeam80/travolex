1. Bangalore to Coorg: The Coffee Lover's Paradise


Distance: 260 km | Best Time: October to March

Want to take in the winding roads with their lines of coffee plantations and misty hills? Then, you must do the Bangalore-Coorg road trip. Coorg is a favorite destination for young travelers when it comes to serene beauty, touted as the 'Scotland of India'. Lush green landscapes, cool climate, and spots like Abbey Falls for waterfalls form an enthralling trip. Thrilling treks at Tadiandamol and white water rafting at Barapole add to the adventure.
Don't Miss: Take a visit to the coffee plantations, the seat of Raja, and the wild at Nagarhole National Park.



2. Chennai to Pondicherry: The Ultimate Beachside Adventure


Distance: 160 km | Best Time: November to February

If you are a beach and vibrant culture lover, this is your route from Chennai to Pondicherry. This East Coast Road or ECR road trip is literally a dream with Bay of Bengal views on one side. 3. Bangalore to Ooty: The Hills Are Calling

Distance: 270 km | Best Time: All year round

The journey from Bangalore to Ooty won't let you down if you enjoy hill towns and warm weather. Along the route, you may see wildlife like deer and elephants as you pass through the Bandipur Forest Reserve. The hazy, meandering roads that lead up to Ooty provide breathtaking vistas of valleys, tea gardens, and buildings from the colonial past.
For expansive vistas, don't miss Doddabetta Peak, Ooty Lake, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.



4. Mangalore to Gokarna: The Ultimate Chill Vibes

Distance: 144 km | Best Time: October to March

Seeking a less crowded beachside experience than Goa? The solution is a road trip from Mangalore to Gokarna. Gokarna has become well-known for its immaculate beaches and is becoming more and more popular with youthful travelers looking for a more laid-back beach experience. The drive is a memorable experience because it passes along the gorgeous coastline of Karnataka.
Om Beach is a must-see for its distinctive shape, Half Moon Beach's serenity, and the sunsets over the cliff.



5. Bangalore to Hampi: Time Travel to the Past

Distance: 340 km | Best Time: October to February

The journey from Bangalore to Hampi by car will take you back in time if you enjoy a blend of culture, history, and beautiful scenery. You will arrive in Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site recognized for its historic temples, ruins, and rock formations, as you travel through rural Karnataka. Young adventurers who love adventure and absorbing in the atmosphere of the past will love this tour.
The renowned Virupaksha Temple, the terrain dotted with boulders, and the sunset vistas from Matanga Hill are not to be missed.



6. Kerala Road Trip: Kochi to Munnar


Distance: 130 km | Best Time: September to March

In South India, Kerala, sometimes known as "God's Own Country," offers some of the most breathtaking road excursions. Traveling by car from Kochi to Munnar offers you breath-taking views of tea gardens, waterfalls, and winding mountain roads. Munnar is the ideal place for young visitors and environment enthusiasts looking for an Instagram-worthy vacation because of its gorgeous plantations and pleasant climate.
The tea gardens, Eravikulam National Park, and Mattupetty Dam are must-see destinations.



7. Bangalore to Kodaikanal: The Princess of Hill Stations

Distance: 465 km | Best Time: October to March

Worth taking are those that consider an epic drive with topography comprising mountains and valleys all the way from Bangalore to Kodaikanal. The long drive pays off as you come across scenic landscapes, rolling hills, waterfalls, and dense forests. Upon reaching Kodaikanal, one can engage in boating, trekking, and some viewpoints that would spellbind you.
Full Day: Kodaikanal Lake-a very famous man-made lake; Coaker's Walk; Dolphin's Nose viewpoint.
