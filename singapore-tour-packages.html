  • Day 1

    Arrival in Singapore

    Arrive at Changi International Airport and transfer to your hotel. Relax or explore the city’s nightlife at Clarke Quay.

  • Day 2

    Singapore City Tour

    Visit Merlion Park, Marina Bay Sands, Chinatown, Little India, and the National Orchid Garden. Spend your afternoon shopping on Orchard Road.
  • Day 3

    Sentosa Island Adventure

    Enjoy a fun-filled day at Sentosa Island - featuring S.E.A. Aquarium, Madame Tussauds, Adventure Cove Waterpark, and the Wings of Time show.
  • Day 4

    Gardens by the Bay and Marina Bay Sands

    Explore Gardens by the Bay, including the Flower Dome and Cloud Forest. Later, visit Marina Bay Sands SkyPark for stunning city views and the Spectra Light Show.
  • Day 5

    Wildlife & Nature Exploration

    Experience the Singapore Zoo, River Wonders, and the world-famous Night Safari for a unique wildlife encounter.
  • Day 6

    Bird Paradise & Kampong Glam

    Visit Bird Paradise and see over 3,500 exotic birds. Later, explore Kampong Glam, Sultan Mosque, and Haji Lane for a taste of local culture.
  • Day 7

    Shopping & Leisure Day

    Free day to shop at Orchard Road, Marina Bay Shoppes, or Jewel Changi Airport. Enjoy a spa session or high tea before your final evening in Singapore.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Singapore

    After breakfast, transfer to Changi Airport for your flight home - taking with you the memories of a perfect Singapore holiday.
