  • Day 1

    Arrival in Singapore

    Arrive at Changi International Airport, where a Travolex representative will welcome you and assist with your transfer to the hotel. After check-in, spend the rest of the day relaxing or exploring your local neighborhood. If time permits, consider a visit to Clarke Quay in the evening for riverside dining and a first taste of Singapore's vibrant nightlife.

  • Day 2

    Singapore City Tour

    Begin your day with a guided city tour to discover Singapore’s rich cultural diversity and modern skyline. Visit the iconic Merlion Park for a photo stop with the Marina Bay Sands in the background, followed by a drive past the historic Padang and the colonial-era Civic District. Explore the beautiful Thian Hock Keng Temple in Chinatown and walk through the bustling streets of Little India. End the tour at the National Orchid Garden, located within the lush Singapore Botanic Gardens—a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Your afternoon is free for leisure or optional shopping on Orchard Road.
  • Day 3

    Sentosa Island Adventure

    After breakfast, set out for a full day at Sentosa, Singapore’s premier island resort. Begin with a scenic cable car ride offering panoramic views of the harbor. Visit attractions such as Madame Tussauds, S.E.A. Aquarium (one of the world’s largest), and the Trick Eye Museum. In the afternoon, unwind on the island’s white sandy beaches or visit Adventure Cove Waterpark. End your Sentosa experience with the Wings of Time night show, a magical display of water, lights, and fireworks.
  • Day 4

    Gardens by the Bay and Marina Bay Sands

    Today, discover Singapore’s iconic green spaces and futuristic architecture. Start with a visit to Gardens by the Bay, where you’ll explore the climate-controlled Flower Dome and Cloud Forest, featuring exotic plants and a man-made indoor waterfall. Later, walk among the towering Supertrees in the outdoor garden and enjoy views from the OCBC Skyway. In the evening, head to Marina Bay Sands SkyPark for breathtaking city views. If you like, stay to enjoy the Spectra Light and Water Show by the bay.
  • Day 5

    Wildlife & Nature Exploration

    This day begins with a morning visit to the Singapore Zoo, renowned for its open-concept enclosures and immersive wildlife experiences. Optional breakfast with orangutans can be arranged. Later in the afternoon, visit the River Wonders, Asia’s first river-themed wildlife park. For a magical evening, embark on a Night Safari, a unique open-air zoo experience where you’ll see nocturnal animals in their naturalistic habitats under subtle lighting.
  • Day 6

    Jurong Bird Paradise & Cultural Immersion

    After breakfast, explore Bird Paradise, Singapore’s newest bird park featuring over 3,500 birds across themed aviaries. Enjoy interactive feeding sessions and walk-through habitats. In the afternoon, delve deeper into the city’s multicultural fabric with a guided heritage walk through Kampong Glam, home to the majestic Sultan Mosque and vibrant Haji Lane. Learn about Malay-Muslim traditions and enjoy some local snacks or try your hand at batik painting in a local workshop.
  • Day 7

    Shopping & Leisure Day

    This is a free day for shopping, relaxation, or optional tours. Spend time at Orchard Road, Singapore’s retail haven, or explore designer boutiques and local brands in Marina Bay Sands’ Shoppes. Alternatively, visit Jewel Changi Airport to experience its stunning Rain Vortex waterfall, Canopy Park, and unique shopping outlets. You may also opt for a spa treatment or high tea in one of Singapore’s luxury hotels to end your trip on a serene note.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Singapore

    After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Changi International Airport for your onward flight. Depending on your departure time, you might squeeze in some last-minute shopping or enjoy a farewell coffee with views of the Singapore skyline.
