  • Day 1

    Arrival in Rome – The Eternal City

    Arrive at Rome’s Fiumicino Airport, where a Travolex representative will greet you and assist with your hotel transfer. After check-in, take the evening to explore the cobbled streets, sip espresso at a local café, or simply admire the Colosseum from a distance.

  • Day 2

    Rome – Ancient Wonders and Vatican City

    Begin your day with a guided tour of Ancient Rome, including the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill. In the afternoon, visit Vatican City, where you'll explore St. Peter’s Basilica, the Vatican Museums, and the breathtaking Sistine Chapel, home to Michelangelo’s ceiling masterpiece.
  • Day 3

    Rome to Florence – The Cradle of the Renaissance

    After breakfast, travel by high-speed train or private vehicle to Florence, the capital of Tuscany. Upon arrival, enjoy a walking tour covering the Duomo, Giotto’s Bell Tower, Piazza della Signoria, and the historic Ponte Vecchio. Optionally, visit the Uffizi Gallery or the Accademia Gallery to see Michelangelo’s David.
  • Day 4

    Florence – Day Trip to Pisa & Tuscan Countryside

    Embark on a half-day trip to Pisa to see the world-famous Leaning Tower, along with the Cathedral and Baptistery in the Piazza dei Miracoli. Return through the scenic Tuscan countryside, with an optional wine tasting experience in Chianti.
  • Day 5

    Florence to Venice – The Floating City

    Depart Florence and head to Venice, a city like no other. Check in at your hotel and then enjoy a guided walking tour of St. Mark’s Square, St. Mark’s Basilica, and the Doge’s Palace. Wander through the labyrinth of canals and alleys, or indulge in a gondola ride for the quintessential Venetian experience.
  • Day 6

    Venice – Islands of Murano & Burano

    Today, take a half-day excursion to the charming islands of Murano, famous for its glass-blowing artisans, and Burano, known for its lace-making and brightly colored houses. The afternoon is free to shop, relax, or explore more of Venice’s hidden gems.
  • Day 7

    Venice to Milan – Fashion and Architecture

    Travel to Milan, Italy’s fashion capital and a city of impressive modernity mixed with classic charm. Visit the magnificent Duomo di Milano, stroll through the elegant Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and see La Scala Opera House. If time permits, view Leonardo da Vinci’s The Last Supper (advance booking required).
  • Day 8

    Departure from Milan - Arrivederci, Italia!

    After breakfast, transfer to Milan Malpensa Airport for your flight home. Leave with unforgettable memories of art, architecture, pasta, and passion.
