Best 3-Day Honeymoon Road Trips from Pune

05 November

If you and your partner are looking for a romantic honeymoon that’s more about experiences than luxury suites, a road trip from Pune might be your perfect love story. With the Western Ghats, misty hill stations, and hidden beaches just a few hours away, Pune offers countless offbeat honeymoon destinations where love and adventure meet.

Whether you’re newly married or simply celebrating togetherness, here are the best 3-day honeymoon road trips from Pune that promise peace, privacy, and postcard-perfect moments.


1. Bhandardara - Lakeside Romance Amid the Sahyadris


Distance: ~170 km | Travel Time: ~5 hours

If candlelight dinners by the lake and starlit skies sound like your idea of romance, Bhandardara is your dream escape. Nestled beside the serene Arthur Lake and surrounded by lush mountains, this charming hill station offers an ideal mix of intimacy and natural beauty.


Things to do:

  • Stay at a lakeside resort or glamping tent
  • Visit Randha Falls and Umbrella Falls
  • Enjoy boating or stargazing near the dam

Why it’s perfect: Secluded, scenic, and unbelievably peaceful perfect for couples who want to disconnect from the world and reconnect with each other.



2. Dapoli - A Hidden Beach Paradise


Distance: ~200 km | Travel Time: ~6 hours

Want to swap city chaos for sea breeze and sunsets? Head to Dapoli, the lesser-known coastal gem of Maharashtra. Its pristine beaches, cozy homestays, and seafood delights make it one of the most intimate coastal getaways near Pune.


Things to do:

  • Explore Murud and Ladghar beaches
  • Visit ancient temples and sea forts
  • Go dolphin watching in the early morning

Why it’s perfect: Peaceful beaches, fewer crowds, and plenty of romantic walks on golden sands.



3. Kaas Plateau & Thoseghar Falls - A Floral Fairytale


Distance: ~140 km | Travel Time: ~4 hours

Known as Maharashtra’s very own “Valley of Flowers,” Kaas Plateau turns into a colorful paradise between August and October. Pair your visit with a stop at Thoseghar Falls for a truly breathtaking honeymoon experience.


Things to do:

  • Walk hand-in-hand through blooming wildflower fields
  • Enjoy a picnic with views of cascading waterfalls
  • Visit the nearby town of Satara for local treats

Why it’s perfect: Nature, colors, and endless photo ops — ideal for couples who love scenic beauty and peaceful escapes.



4. Mulshi & Tamhini Ghat - Nature’s Hideout


Distance: ~50 km | Travel Time: ~2 hours

For couples short on time but big on love, Mulshi Dam and Tamhini Ghat make for an easy yet dreamy honeymoon road trip from Pune. Expect emerald-green valleys, misty waterfalls, and cozy hillside cottages.


Things to do:

  • Stay in a luxury eco-resort
  • Visit Tamhini waterfalls and Devkund Lake
  • Go kayaking or enjoy a nature walk

Why it’s perfect: Close to Pune, romantic weather year-round, and ideal for couples looking for a quick yet meaningful getaway.



5. Amboli - The Whispering Hill Station


Distance: ~340 km | Travel Time: ~8 hours

Hidden deep in the Sahyadris, Amboli is one of Maharashtra’s most underrated honeymoon spots. Misty forests, winding roads, and old-world charm make it a dreamy destination for couples who crave seclusion.


Things to do:

  • Visit Amboli Falls and Hiranyakeshi Temple
  • Go for forest walks and sunset viewpoints
  • Enjoy hot chai in the chill mountain breeze

Why it’s perfect: Less commercialized, refreshingly green, and blissfully romantic.



Travel Tips for Your 3-Day Honeymoon Road Trip


  • Plan your route: Use Google Maps or offline navigation; some mountain areas lose network coverage.
  • Pack light but smart: Include warm clothes, first-aid kit, and rain gear (especially during monsoon).
  • Book stays in advance: Opt for boutique stays or eco-resorts for a more intimate experience.
  • Keep cash handy: Many remote areas have limited ATMs or online payment options.

Final Thoughts


Love doesn’t always need grand gestures sometimes, it’s just a scenic drive, shared laughter, and endless views. These offbeat, intimate 3-day honeymoon road trips from Pune are perfect for couples who want to celebrate love in its purest form - simple, soulful, and spontaneous.

So, pack your bags, play your favorite playlist, and let the journey become your most romantic story yet.
