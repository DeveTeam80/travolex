Let's explore It:

Day 1: Cairo Arrival and City Tour


Once you arrive in Cairo, immerse yourself in the city's vibrant energy. Check into your accommodation, then take a guided tour of the city's most prominent landmarks. Visit the Great Pyramids of Giza and marvel at the ancient engineering feats. Capture amazing moments as you stand in front of the Sphinx, the pyramids' mystery guardian. Continue your journey to the Egyptian Museum, which houses a treasure trove of antiquities that showcase ancient Egypt's rich history


Day 2: Cairo: A Day of Exploration


Begin your day with a visit to Saqqara Necropolis, the world's oldest known monumental cemetery. Explore Djoser's Step Pyramid, the earliest large-scale stone pyramid ever built. Next, visit the Coptic Cairo neighborhood, which is a remarkable blend of Christian and Islamic elements. Explore the Ben Ezra A mosque, one of the

world's oldest mosques

, as well as the Hanging Church, a one-of-a-kind monument erected on top of a Roman fort.


Day 3: Cairo to Luxor


A journey through time. Take a picturesque train ride from Cairo to Luxor, a city rich in history. After arriving, check into your accommodation and unwind before embarking on a Nile River cruise. As you ride by the beautiful Luxor temples, relax with a relaxing meal on board.


Day 4: Luxor:


Temples & Tombs Wake up to spectacular views of the Nile and begin your day by visiting the Karnak Temple Complex, one of the

world's largest ancient religious sites

. Admire the towering hypostyle hall and visit the many temples within the complex. In the afternoon, tour the Valley of the Kings, which contains the tombs of several pharaohs. Explore the artistically adorned tombs and discover ancient Egyptian ideas about the afterlife.


Day 5: Luxor to Aswan:


A River Cruise Adventure. Continue your Nile River cruise to Aswan, a city renowned for its breathtaking scenery and ancient buildings. Enjoy the river's calm splendor as you travel past lush green meadows and historic settlements.


Day 6: Aswan:


A Day of Exploration Explore the ancient Temple of Philae, which is devoted to the goddess Isis. This majestic temple is situated on a little island in the center of the Nile River. Take a boat journey to the temple and marvel at the beautiful sculptures and statues. In the afternoon, go to the Unfinished Obelisk, a gigantic granite obelisk that was never completed.


Day 7: Aswan to Cairo:


Farewell to Egypt. Take an airplane or train from Aswan to Cairo. Spend your final day in Cairo exploring local markets or seeing any other sights on your itinerary. Enjoy a farewell meal featuring excellent Egyptian food.


Tips for Your Egyptian Adventure



  • Dress modestly: When visiting religious sites, cover your shoulders and knees.
  • Learn a few basic Arabic phrases: This will go a long way in interacting with locals and making your trip more enjoyable.
  • Bargain at markets: Haggling is a common practice in Egypt. Don't be afraid to negotiate for a fair price.
  • Stay hydrated: The Egyptian climate can be hot, so make sure to drink plenty of water.
  • Respect local customs: Be mindful of local customs and traditions.
