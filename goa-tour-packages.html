  • Day 1

    Arrival in Goa – Welcome to Paradise

    Upon arrival at Goa Airport or Railway Station, you'll be greeted and transferred to your hotel or resort for check-in, where you can unwind and relax after your journey. In the evening, head out to a nearby beach such as Calangute or Baga to enjoy a picturesque sunset, with the option to explore lively beachside cafes or local markets before returning for an overnight stay in North Goa.

  • Day 2

    North Goa Sightseeing

    after a hearty breakfast, you'll set out to explore the highlights of North Goa. The day’s sightseeing includes iconic landmarks such as Fort Aguada, with its sweeping views of the Arabian Sea, and a series of beautiful beaches including Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, and Vagator. Each beach has its own charm—whether it's the lively shacks at Baga or the scenic cliffs at Vagator. Adventure lovers can opt for water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, or banana boat rides. As the sun sets, you may relax at a beach shack or explore Goa’s vibrant nightlife. You'll return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    South Goa Exploration

    Day three is dedicated to the cultural and scenic beauty of South Goa. After breakfast, you'll head out to visit the historic churches of Old Goa, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral, both rich in Portuguese heritage. The day continues with visits to Miramar Beach and the Dona Paula viewpoint, followed by a visit to Mangueshi Temple, one of the oldest and most respected Hindu temples in Goa. In the evening, you can enjoy a more tranquil beach experience at Colva or Palolem. You can choose to either stay overnight in South Goa or return to your hotel in the north, depending on your accommodation preference.

  • Day 4

    Adventure & Leisure

    On day four, after breakfast, you have multiple options to choose from, depending on your interests. You can go on an adventurous trip to Dudhsagar Waterfalls, a monsoon-season spectacle surrounded by lush forests. Alternatively, enjoy a dolphin-watching cruise or take a serene island-hopping boat tour. A visit to a traditional spice plantation with an authentic Goan lunch is also a great cultural experience. The rest of the evening is free for you to relax, enjoy your resort, or explore Panjim’s charming markets and cafes. You’ll spend the night in Goa.

  • Day 5

    Free Day – Beach, Spa & Nightlife

    After breakfast, you can choose to spend your time unwinding on the beach, indulging in a rejuvenating spa treatment, or shopping for souvenirs in the local flea markets. If you're feeling lively, Goa’s vibrant nightlife awaits—with beach parties, live music at shacks, or a glamorous night at a floating casino like Deltin Royale or Big Daddy. For a more laid-back evening, enjoy seafood at a beachside restaurant while listening to the waves. Return to your hotel for the final overnight stay.

  • Day 6

    Departure

    On the sixth and final day, enjoy your breakfast and check out from the hotel. You’ll be transferred to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. As you leave Goa, you’ll carry with you sun-kissed memories, vibrant experiences, and a refreshing break that only Travolex can offer.
