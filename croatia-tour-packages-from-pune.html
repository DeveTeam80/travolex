  • Day 1

    Arrival in Zagreb - The Heart of Croatia

    Arrive at Franjo Tuđman Airport and meet your Travolex representative. Transfer to your hotel and relax. In the evening, explore Ban Jelačić Square and the lively Tkalčićeva Street filled with cafes, restaurants, and boutiques.
  • Day 2

    Zagreb City Tour - Explore the Old Town

    Enjoy a guided walking tour through Zagreb’s Old Town, visiting landmarks like the Cathedral of Zagreb, Dolac Market, and St. Mark’s Church. Spend the afternoon at leisure exploring museums, galleries, or enjoy coffee at Cvjetni Trg.
  • Day 3

    Plitvice Lakes National Park → Split

    Drive to the UNESCO-listed Plitvice Lakes National Park, known for its cascading waterfalls, wooden boardwalks, and turquoise lakes. Later, continue to Split and enjoy a relaxing evening along the Riva promenade.
  • Day 4

    Split & Diocletian’s Palace

    Explore Split’s ancient Roman heritage at Diocletian’s Palace, Peristyle Square, and the Marjan Hill viewpoints. Optionally, join a Blue Cave or Blue Lagoon speedboat tour for a thrilling island adventure (seasonal).
  • Day 5

    Island Day Trip – Hvar (optional Korčula or Brač)

    Take a ferry to Hvar Island, famous for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant nightlife. Visit Fortica Fortress, wander through cobblestone streets, and relax at the Pakleni Islands. Return to Split by evening.
  • Day 6

    Split → Dubrovnik - The Pearl of the Adriatic

    Enjoy a scenic coastal drive to Dubrovnik, one of the most beautiful cities on the Adriatic Coast. Take a sunset stroll along Stradun Street in the UNESCO-listed Old Town.
  • Day 7

    Dubrovnik City Walls & Cable Car Experience

    Walk along the iconic Dubrovnik City Walls, explore Fort Lovrijenac, and take the cable car to Mount Srđ for panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and Old Town.
  • Day 8

    Departure from Dubrovnik

    After breakfast, transfer to Dubrovnik Airport for your return flight. Optionally, enjoy a coffee by the harbor before departure.
