2. Cable Car Ride: A Scenic Adventure

Auli boasts the second-longest cable car ride in Asia, stretching 4 km between Joshimath and Auli. The ride offers breathtaking views of the snow-laden landscapes and towering peaks.

Highlights:

  • Panoramic views of the Garhwal Himalayas.
  • An unforgettable 25-minute journey.
  • A unique way to witness Auli’s winter wonderland.

Don’t miss this must-do activity, and let Travolex book your cable car tickets in advance.

3. Trekking Adventures

December is perfect for trekking enthusiasts looking to explore Auli’s snowy trails. Popular trekking routes include:

  • Gorson Bugyal Trek:A 3 km trek offering mesmerizing views of Nanda Devi and other peaks.
  • Kuari Pass Trek: A moderate trek that takes you through dense forests and meadows.
  • Chattrakund Trek:Ideal for beginners, this 1 km trek leads to a serene lake surrounded by snow
