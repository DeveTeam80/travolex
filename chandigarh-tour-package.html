  • Day 1

    Arrival in Chandigarh - India’s City Beautiful

    Arrive at Chandigarh Airport or Railway Station and check in at your hotel. Spend your evening at Sector 17 Plaza, enjoying shopping, dining, and nightlife in the city’s vibrant heart.

  • Day 2

    City Tour - Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake & Capitol Complex

    Visit Nek Chand’s Rock Garden, the iconic Sukhna Lake, and the UNESCO-listed Capitol Complex featuring the Open Hand Monument and Le Corbusier’s masterpieces. Enjoy sunset by the lake before returning to your hotel.
  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Pinjore Gardens & Timber Trail

    Take a short excursion to Pinjore Gardens for Mughal-style charm and continue to Timber Trail for a scenic cable car ride over the Shivalik Hills. Evening free for shopping at Elante Mall.
  • Day 4

    Departure from Chandigarh - Until Next Time

    After breakfast, depart with memories of Chandigarh’s modern elegance, lush greenery, and cultural vibrance.
