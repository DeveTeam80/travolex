  • Day 1

    Arrival in Chandigarh – India’s City Beautiful

    Your journey begins upon arrival at Chandigarh Airport or Railway Station, where a Travolex representative welcomes you and escorts you to your hotel. After check-in and some rest, head out in the evening to explore the well-planned charm of Sector 17 Plaza, Chandigarh’s main commercial hub, known for shopping, dining, and vibrant nightlife. Relax at a café or take a stroll among fountains and open-air seating areas. Return to your hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Chandigarh.

  • Day 2

    City Tour – Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake & Capitol Complex

    After breakfast, set out to explore the best of Chandigarh. Begin with a visit to Nek Chand’s Rock Garden, an extraordinary sculpture park built entirely from industrial and domestic waste materials. Wander through its maze of waterfalls, art installations, and mosaic-covered walls. From there, walk over to the tranquil Sukhna Lake, a man-made reservoir ideal for boating, cycling, or simply relaxing by the waterfront.Post lunch, head to the Chandigarh Capitol Complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site designed by legendary architect Le Corbusier. Visit landmarks like the Open Hand Monument, High Court, and Legislative Assembly (external viewing subject to permissions). End your day with a sunset view by the lake before returning to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Pinjore Gardens & Timber Trail

    Today, embark on a scenic drive to Pinjore Gardens (Yadavindra Gardens), located about 25 km from Chandigarh. These 17th-century Mughal-style gardens are beautifully laid out in terraces with fountains, pavilions, and blooming flora—perfect for photography or a quiet morning stroll. Later, head towards Timber Trail near Parwanoo for an exciting cable car ride (optional), offering panoramic views of the Shivalik Hills. Enjoy lunch at the hilltop café before returning to Chandigarh in the afternoon. Spend your evening at leisure or explore more shopping in Elante Mall, one of North India's largest malls. Overnight stay in Chandigarh.

  • Day 4

    Departure from Chandigarh – Until Next Time

    After breakfast and check-out, a Travolex representative will assist with your transfer to the airport or railway station. As you leave the City Beautiful, take with you memories of artistic innovation, scenic landscapes, and the perfectly balanced rhythm of urban planning and nature.
