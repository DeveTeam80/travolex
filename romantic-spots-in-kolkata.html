1. Prinsep Ghat: A Riverside Romance


Prinsep Ghat is one of the most iconic and romantic places in Kolkata, offering couples a serene setting by the Hooghly River. The historic colonial-style pavilion, coupled with the breathtaking view of the Vidyasagar Setu (Second Hooghly Bridge), creates an enchanting atmosphere.


Activities for Couples:

  • Enjoy a romantic walk along the beautifully lit promenade.
  • Take a boat ride on the river during sunset for a magical experience.
  • Indulge in street food while enjoying the gentle breeze.

Whether it’s a quiet evening or a memorable date, Prinsep Ghat is a timeless spot for couples. Travolex can assist in organizing a perfect itinerary to include this charming location.

2. Eco Park: Nature’s Retreat for Lovebirds

Eco Park is a sprawling urban park offering a plethora of activities for couples who enjoy nature and adventure. With its lush greenery, serene lakes, and thematic gardens, this park is a favourite destination for couples in Kolkata.


Must-Visit Attractions:

  • The Japanese Garden for a peaceful retreat.
  • The Butterfly Garden for a romantic stroll amidst colourful flora.
  • The Floating Musical Fountain for an awe-inspiring experience.

For those seeking adventure, tandem cycling and boating options are available. Whether you prefer an action-packed day or a leisurely outing, Eco Park caters to all. Travolex offers tailored travel packages that include this vibrant destination.

Romantic Highlights:
