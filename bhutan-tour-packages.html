  • Day 1

    Arrival in Paro - Transfer to Thimphu

    Arrive at Paro International Airport and drive to Thimphu, the capital city of Bhutan. Enjoy scenic views of mountain valleys and rivers en route. In the evening, stroll along Norzin Lam Street and experience Bhutan’s peaceful local charm.
  • Day 2

    Thimphu Sightseeing

    Visit key attractions like the National Memorial Chorten, Buddha Dordenma, and Tashichho Dzong. Explore the Folk Heritage Museum and Textile Museum to learn about Bhutanese art, culture, and spirituality.
  • Day 3

    Thimphu to Punakha via Dochula Pass

    Drive to Punakha through the scenic Dochula Pass (3,100m), offering views of the eastern Himalayas. Visit Punakha Dzong, Bhutan’s most beautiful fortress, and hike to Chimi Lhakhang, the fertility temple dedicated to the Divine Madman.
  • Day 4

    Punakha to Paro - Leisure and Exploration

    Return to Paro, stopping en route for photos and tea. Visit Rinpung Dzong, stroll through Paro town, and enjoy optional experiences like a traditional hot stone bath for relaxation.
  • Day 5

    Paro - Hike to Tiger’s Nest Monastery

    Hike to Taktsang Monastery (Tiger’s Nest), Bhutan’s most sacred site perched dramatically on a cliffside. Enjoy stunning views, spiritual peace, and traditional Bhutanese lunch after the hike. Evening at leisure or cultural dinner at a local farmhouse.
  • Day 6

    Departure from Paro

    After breakfast, transfer to Paro International Airport for departure, carrying home unforgettable memories of Bhutan’s beauty and tranquility.
