  • Day 1

    Arrival in Darjeeling – Into the Himalayas

    Your journey to the "Queen of the Hills" begins with your arrival at either Bagdogra Airport or New Jalpaiguri (NJP) Railway Station. Upon arrival, you’ll be greeted by our representative and transferred to Darjeeling. As you ascend through winding roads and lush greenery, you’ll feel the crisp mountain air welcome you. After checking in at your hotel, the remainder of the day is at leisure. You can choose to rest or take a quiet stroll through Mall Road, soaking in the cool breeze and colonial charm. Spend the night at your hotel in Darjeeling.

  • Day 2

    Sunrise & Sightseeing in Darjeeling

    On Day 2, rise early for a once-in-a-lifetime experience — a sunrise visit to Tiger Hill. Watching the first light of dawn strike the peaks of Mt. Kanchenjunga is unforgettable. On the way back, you’ll visit the iconic Batasia Loop and Ghoom Monastery. After breakfast at the hotel, the rest of the day will be spent exploring the local highlights of Darjeeling. Attractions include the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, the Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park (home to the red panda), Tenzing Rock, and the Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre. You’ll also have time to visit a tea estate for a guided tour and tasting session. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Leisure & Toy Train Ride

    Day 3 is designed for relaxation and local flavor. After a leisurely breakfast, enjoy a peaceful day visiting lesser-known spots or indulging in personal interests. You may take a ride on the UNESCO World Heritage Darjeeling Himalayan Railway Toy Train, a nostalgic journey through picturesque landscapes. Later, you can explore local markets and pick up handcrafted souvenirs, Tibetan curios, and Darjeeling tea. The evening is yours to enjoy—perhaps with a cup of fresh tea and a view of the mountains. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 4

    Excursion to Kalimpong

    >On Day 4, after breakfast, you’ll embark on a scenic drive to Kalimpong, a quiet hill station just two hours from Darjeeling. Upon arrival, explore the town’s Buddhist monasteries, colonial architecture, and flower nurseries. Visit the Zang Dhok Palri Phodang Monastery and enjoy panoramic views from Deolo Hill. You’ll also stop by Durpin Dara and local markets before heading to your hotel in Kalimpong for the night. This day offers a slower, more spiritual experience in the eastern Himalayas.

  • Day 5

    Free Day to Unwind

    Day 5 offers a mix of culture and leisure. After breakfast, take some time to explore the peaceful town of Kalimpong at your own pace. If you’re interested, Travolex can arrange visits to nearby villages for authentic cultural experiences. Later in the day, you’ll head back to Darjeeling or optionally stay overnight in Kalimpong, depending on your itinerary preferences. Enjoy your final evening in the hills with a quiet dinner and some stargazing.

  • Day 6

    Departure

    On Day 6, after breakfast, it’s time to check out. You’ll be transferred back to Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station for your onward journey, carrying with you the serene energy of the Himalayas, the fragrance of tea gardens, and unforgettable memories of your trip with Travolex.
