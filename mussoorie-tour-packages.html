  • Day 1

    Arrival in Dehradun – Transfer to Mussoorie

    Upon arrival at Leh’s Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport on Day 1, guests are transferred to their hotel where they’re welcomed with a traditional drink. The first day is reserved for acclimatization, given Leh’s high altitude. In the evening, light exploration includes a visit to the peaceful Shanti Stupa and the vibrant Leh Market for a taste of the local culture and shopping.

  • Day 2

    Mussoorie Local Sightseeing

    Day 2 is dedicated to exploring the outskirts of Leh along the Srinagar-Leh highway. Guests will visit Magnetic Hill, a gravity-defying phenomenon, followed by the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar Rivers at Sangam Point. The day also includes stops at Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, an important spiritual site, and the Hall of Fame Museum, which honors India’s brave soldiers. The scenic drive and cultural immersion make this day both enriching and visually stunning.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Dhanaulti

    On Day 3, guests embark on a day trip to the serene town of Dhanaulti, located about 25 kilometers from Mussoorie. The drive itself is a visual treat, winding through tall deodar forests. In Dhanaulti, travelers can unwind at the Eco Park, visit the ancient Surkanda Devi Temple, and, for those seeking a thrill, take part in adventure activities like ziplining or rappelling. By evening, they return to Mussoorie for a relaxed dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Landour Exploration

    Day 4 offers a more intimate experience of the hills with a visit to the colonial-era town of Landour. Here, guests can walk to Lal Tibba, the highest point in the region, and enjoy local delicacies at iconic cafés in Sisters Bazaar. St. Paul’s Church adds a touch of heritage to the journey, making Landour a perfect blend of nostalgia and nature. The rest of the day is kept free for guests to enjoy at their own pace, whether strolling through pine-covered trails or unwinding at their hotel.

  • Day 5

    Departure

    On Day 5, after breakfast and check-out, guests are driven back to Dehradun for their departure. The Mussoorie trip concludes with memories of cool breezes, mountain walks, and the timeless charm of the Himalayas.
