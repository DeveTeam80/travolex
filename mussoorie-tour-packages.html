  • Day 1

    Arrival in Dehradun – Transfer to Mussoorie

    Arrive at Dehradun Airport/Railway Station and drive to Mussoorie through scenic mountain roads. Check in and relax. In the evening, stroll along Mall Road or enjoy serene views from Gun Hill Point. Overnight stay in Mussoorie.

  • Day 2

    Mussoorie Local Sightseeing

    After breakfast, explore Mussoorie’s top attractions including Kempty Falls, Company Garden, Camel’s Back Road, Gun Hill, and Mussoorie Lake. Spend the evening shopping or enjoying cafés on Mall Road. Overnight in Mussoorie.

  • Day 3

    Day Trip to Dhanaulti

    Drive to the peaceful hill town of Dhanaulti. Visit the Eco Park, Surkanda Devi Temple, and enjoy optional adventure activities like ziplining or rappelling. Return to Mussoorie for a relaxed evening. Overnight stay.

  • Day 4

    Landour Exploration

    Visit the charming colonial town of Landour. Explore Lal Tibba, Sisters Bazaar, St. Paul’s Church, and cozy hillside cafés. Enjoy free time for nature walks or leisure. Overnight in Mussoorie.

  • Day 5

    Departure

    After breakfast, check out and transfer to Dehradun Airport/Railway Station for your return journey with refreshing Himalayan memories.
