Australia

Australia, a

land of striking contrasts

and endless adventures, is a

dream destination for travelers

seeking both natural beauty and urban sophistication. Whether you're admiring the sails of the Sydney Opera House, exploring the sacred lands of Uluru, or diving into the colorful ecosystems of the Great Barrier Reef, Australia never ceases to amaze. The country boasts vast deserts, lush rainforests, cosmopolitan cities, and vibrant Aboriginal culture. From the bustling streets of Melbourne and Sydney to the red heart of the Outback and coastal escapes like the Gold Coast and Whitsundays, every region presents a unique travel story.

Australia is ideal for family holidays

, solo travel, adventure tours, and luxury escapes—all supported by world-class infrastructure and diverse experiences.

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Sydney

    Upon arrival at Sydney International Airport, guests will be warmly greeted by a Travolex representative and transferred to their hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free for guests to relax or explore the city at their own pace. Sydney’s waterfront, restaurants, and shopping precincts offer a great start to the Australian adventure. Overnight stay will be in Sydney.

  • Day 2

    Sydney City Tour and Bondi Beach

    After breakfast, the day begins with a guided city tour of Sydney. Guests will visit iconic landmarks such as the Sydney Opera House, Sydney Harbour Bridge, Darling Harbour, and The Rocks. A scenic drive will follow through upscale areas like Kings Cross and Double Bay, concluding at the world-famous Bondi Beach. Guests will have time to stroll along the beach promenade or enjoy a coffee with a sea view. The afternoon is free for shopping or optional experiences such as the Sydney Tower Eye or a Harbour Cruise. Overnight stay will be in Sydney.
  • Day 3

    Blue Mountains and Wildlife Park

    This day is dedicated to a full-day excursion to the Blue Mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Guests will enjoy breathtaking views of the Jamison Valley and the iconic Three Sisters rock formation. The itinerary includes a visit to Scenic World, where optional rides like the Scenic Skyway or Railway offer dramatic views. On the way back, guests will stop at a wildlife park to interact with native animals like kangaroos and koalas. Return to Sydney in the evening for overnight stay.
  • Day 4

    Sydney to Gold Coast – Leisure

    After breakfast, guests will be transferred to the airport for a domestic flight to the Gold Coast. On arrival, they will be taken to their hotel, ideally located near the famous Surfers Paradise beach. The rest of the day is free to enjoy the beach, nearby cafes, or simply unwind at the resort. Overnight stay will be in the Gold Coast.
  • Day 5

    Theme Park Fun – Choice of Movie World or Sea World

    Guests will enjoy a full day at one of Australia’s most loved theme parks. They can choose between Warner Bros. Movie World, with its thrilling rides and superhero experiences, or Sea World, where marine life meets entertainment. From roller coasters to dolphin shows, it’s a fun-filled day for families and thrill-seekers alike. Guests return to the hotel in the evening. Overnight stay will be in the Gold Coast.
  • Day 6

    Gold Coast to Cairns – Leisure

    After breakfast, guests will be transferred to the airport for their flight to Cairns, the gateway to the Great Barrier Reef. Upon arrival, they will be met and transferred to their hotel. The remainder of the day is at leisure. Guests may explore the Cairns Esplanade or relax by the lagoon pool. Overnight stay will be in Cairns.
  • Day 7

    Great Barrier Reef Cruise

    This day is dedicated to one of the world’s natural wonders – the Great Barrier Reef. Guests will embark on a full-day reef cruise that includes a semi-submarine ride, snorkelling, underwater viewing, and a buffet lunch onboard. They will visit a reef pontoon, where they can choose to snorkel, relax, or upgrade to activities like scuba diving or a helicopter ride (optional). After a day full of marine adventure and stunning views, guests return to Cairns in the evening. Overnight stay will be in Cairns.
  • Day 8

    Cairns to Melbourne – City Orientation

    Following breakfast, guests will board a domestic flight to Melbourne. Upon arrival, they will be taken on a panoramic city orientation tour covering major sights like Federation Square, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Royal Botanic Gardens, Flinders Street Station, and the Melbourne Cricket Ground (view from outside). After hotel check-in, the rest of the day is at leisure. Guests may explore Melbourne’s hidden laneways, cafes, or take a tram ride through the city. Overnight stay will be in Melbourne.
  • Day 9

    Departure from Melbourne

    After breakfast and depending on the departure time, guests will be transferred to Melbourne International Airport for their flight back home. As the memorable journey across Australia comes to an end, Travolex bids farewell, leaving guests with unforgettable experiences and lasting memories.
